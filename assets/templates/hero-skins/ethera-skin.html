Galactic Explorer Skin

Hero Skin Details

Ability Skin: This skin unlocks a unique Hero portrait for your in-game Avatar and also an additional ability while equipped.

Spacetime Jump - Unlocks with Galactic Explorer Skin

Ability Description

Everytime Ethera uses "Paralyze" and jumps to the Front Line, she will regain 40% of the Health she lost in the last 3 seconds. Note* Also appears to work when Ethera uses "Event Horizon"

Ability Info

Ability only available while Galactic Explorer Skin is equipped
