Knight's Armor Skin

Hero Skin Details

Ability Skin: This skin unlocks a unique Hero portrait for your in-game Avatar and also an additional ability while equipped.

Persistent Protection - Unlocks with Knight's Armor Skin

Ability Description

The shields created by Valan's ability will last an additional 3 seconds. If one of the shields is broken by an enemy attack, the hero who had the shield will get a large Speed increase for 3 seconds

Ability Info

Ability only available while Knight's Armor Skin is equipped
