Genie Skin

Hero Skin Details

Ability Skin: This skin unlocks a unique Hero portrait for your in-game Avatar and also an additional ability while equipped.

Genie's Shield - Unlocks with Genie Skin

Ability Description

The first time Aqua takes a normally fatal amount of Damage, the Damage will be neutralized and Aqua will be immune to Physical Damage for 3 seconds

Ability Info

Ability only available while Genie Skin is equipped
