Dodge Rating
Lvl 5 +20

Increases the Dodge Rating. Dodge Rating increases the chance to Dodge an Attack. Only Physical Attacks can be Dodged. This includes not only Attacks from Physical Damage oriented Heroes, but also the Basic Attacks of Magic Damage Heroes.

Level 5 +12.5%

Increases the Hit Recovery Chance. All Attacks have a chance to inflict a momentary stun like effect. This flinching can interrupt Heroes from Attacking or using Abilities. Forcefields increases the chance a hero will avoid this effect.
