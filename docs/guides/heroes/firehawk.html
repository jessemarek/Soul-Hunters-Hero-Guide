Abilities

Scorching Ray - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Firehawk transforms his life-force into flames that scorch the enemies in front of him, dealing Magic Damage-over-Time. However, this ability depletes Firehawk's Health

Ability Stats

  • (+ 18 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1800 Damage
  • Attributes:

From the Ashes - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

When Firehawk's Health is reduced to zero, he will transform into a fireball for 5 seconds. If the fireball takes enough hits within 5 seconds, it will disappear and Firehawk will perish. If not, he will be Revived, Stunning nearby enemies. (Firehawk can be reborn up to 3 times per battle. The first time he is reborn, the fireball can take 4 hits, the second time 3 hits, and the third time 2 hits)

Ability Stats

  • (+ 180 Health per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Will have 18000 Health after being revived
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time
  • Attributes:

Swooping Strike - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Firehawk dives towards the enemy party, dealing Magic Damage to multiple units

Ability Stats

  • (Base 280 + 14 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 1400 Damage
  • Attributes:

Guardians of the Flame - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Firehawk summons 4 small firebirds that fly by his side for 12 seconds, boosting his Physical Attack and giving his Basic Attack an additional AoE Magic Damage effect

Ability Stats

  • (Base 480 + 12 per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Deals a base of 1200 Damage
  • Attributes:

Radiant Wings - Unlocks with Radiant Wings Skin

Ability Description

Health lost due to "Scorching Ray" is reduced by 20%. Firehawk is also immune to Control Abilites for 2 seconds when he activates "Scorching Ray"

Ability Info

Ability only available while Radiant Wings Skin is equipped
