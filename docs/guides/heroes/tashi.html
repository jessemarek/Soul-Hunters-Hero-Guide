Abilities

Fist of Devastation - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Tashi dashes to the weakest enemy, and delivers a quick series of blows. When God-like Focus is activated: Tashi concentrates his Energy into a single powerful punch which the enemy cannot Dodge. The punch causes severe injuries, reducing the enemy's Energy, as well as temporarily reducing their Health Recovery and Movement Speed. The force of the punch even affects enemies standing behind the target, dealing Physical Damage and Knocking them back

Ability Stats

  • (+ 10 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 2000 Damage

Soaring Dragon - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Tashi unleashes a combination of flying kicks dealing Physical Damage to an enemy. When God-like Focus is activated: At the end of the kick combo, Tashi will deliver a earth-shaking strike, dealing AoE Physical Damage and Stunning enemies in range

Ability Stats

  • (+ 15 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1500 Damage
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time

Palm of Fury - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Tashi delivers an open-palmed blow that inflicts Physical Damage and pushes the target back a short distance. When God-like Focus is activated: Target is knocked back to the edge of the battlefield. When he or she hits the edge, the impact will cause AoE Physical Damage

Ability Stats

  • (Base 400 + 20 per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 2000 Damage
    Knock-back Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Knocked back every time

Intense Focus - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

By using ancient techniques Tashi is able to focus his internal Energy while he's standing in place of walking. It's possible to build up to 5 layers of this focused state. Each layer reduces the Damage Tashi takes by 3% and boosts his Physical Attack. When he has built up at least 3 layers, he may enter a state of God-like Focus, which strengthens all of his abilities (this uses up 3 layers). Using Tashi's Ultimate expends all the layers that he currently has. Whenever Tashi moves passively (such as being knocked-back or lifted), all of the layers will also disappear

Ability Stats

  • (Base 200 + 5 Physical Attack per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Each layer increases Physical Attack by 500
