Abilities

Void Blast - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Morfir dashes behind the enemy with the highest Magic Attack and releases a Void Blast that deals huge Magic Damage to the target and enemies around it

Ability Stats

  • (+ 15 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1500 Damage

Touch of Darkness - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Morfir dashes behind the enemy with the highest Magic Attack and attacks him, also dealing Physical Damage and depleting Energy of enemies in a small radius around the target

Ability Stats

  • (+ 14 Damage and Base 59.2 + 0.8 Energy Drain per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1400 Damage
    Enemy unit loses 139.2 Energy

Blade Dance - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Increases Morfir's Physical Attack

Ability Stats

  • (Base 400 + 20 Physical Attack per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Passive: Increase Physical Attack by 2000

Gorgon Shield - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Morfir uses his profound knowledge of magic to protect himself. Increases Magic Armor

Ability Stats

  • (Base 120 + 3 Magic Armor per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Passive: Increase Magic Armor by 300

Static Shield - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

When Morfir uses his "Touch of Darkness" skill to dash to the enemy with the highest Magic Attack, he will cast a magic shield on himself that lowers incoming Magic Damage
