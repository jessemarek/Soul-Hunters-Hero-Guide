Vernos - Ancient Treant


"You tread on sacred ground!"


"You tread on sacred ground!"

Hero Info and Role

Front Line DPS: Can summon various plants which can attack the enemy. Also protects allies with shields and entangles enemies in poisonous vines

Available In

Traveling Treant

Hero

  • Requires 80 to summon
  • Shop appears once 1 Hero reaches 7
  • 7 hero may be traded for Vernos

Stat Growth Rates

Health 82 Health 147
Physical Attack 4.2 Physical Attack 7.5
Magic Attack 5.8 Magic Attack 10.5
Physical Armor 2.2 Physical Armor 3.9
Magic Armor 2.7 Magic Armor 4.8

Hero Medallions

None

Abilities

Weapons of the Forest - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Vernos temporarily runs to the Back Line and summons 3 types of magical plants. Poisonblooms shoot poison at the enemy, dealing Physical Damage. Energy flowers return orbs of Energy to Vernos. Onion-Bombs explode and deal Magic Damage to enemies, but these cost Vernos Energy

Ability Stats

  • (+ 1 Level per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Summons level 100 Poisonblooms
    Summons level 100 Energy Flowers
    Summons level 100 Onion-Bombs

Nature's Shield - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Vernos summons a natural shield around himself or one of his teammates. The shield increases the hero's Physical Armor as well as gradually replenishing their Health-over-Time

Ability Stats

  • (Base -2 + 4 Armor and Base -9.95 + 10 Health per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Increases Physical Armor by 398
    Gain 990.05 Health per second for 8 seconds

Poison Vine - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Vines wrap themselves around enemies within range, entangling them and dealing Damage-over-Time

Ability Stats

  • (Base 76 + 4 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals 396 Damage per second for 4 seconds
    Entangle Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies below level 100 will be Entangled every time

Seed of Life - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Vernos increases the Max Health of the plants he summons when using his Ultimate

Ability Stats

  • (Base 2000 + 50 Health per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Summoned plants' Max Health increased by 5000

Icebloom - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

While using "Weapons of the Forest", Vernos will upgrade Poisonblooms to Iceblooms. When the Poisonblooms are upgraded, they will regain all of their Health, as well as increase their Physical Attack, Physical Hit Bonus and Attack Speed. When the Iceblooms shoot poison at enemies, it will also have a Slowing effect that can be stacked until the enemy is completely Frozen

Ability Stats

  • (Base 1790 + 30 Physical Attack and Base 178 + 3 Physical Hit Bonus per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Iceblooms increase Physical Attack by 2990
    Iceblooms increase Physical Hit Bonus by 298
