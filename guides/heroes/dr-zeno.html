"Delightful! An intelligent choice!"

Abilities

Cerebral Fire - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Dr. Zeno summons a flaming orb that revolves around him, granting him increased Physical and Magic Attack until the end of the wave, as well as restoring some health. He may use this ability multiple times during a wave, having a cumulative effect

Ability Stats

  • (+ 8 Physical Attack and + 8 Magic Attack per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Increase Physical Attack by 800 until the end of the wave
    Increase Magic Attack by 800 until the end of the wave

Laser Cannon - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Sweep the battlefield with a high-powered laser, dealing AoE Magic Damage

Ability Stats

  • (+ 8 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 800 Damage

Corrosive Slime - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Dr. Zeno fires a ball of slime from his weapon, sticking to one or more enemies. The slime deals Magic Damage and is guaranteed to Slow affected enemies

Ability Stats

  • (Base 240 + 12 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 1200 Damage

Mental Forcefield - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Dr. Zeno focuses his enormous mental energy on protecting his teammates, increasing the Magic Armor of all allies

Ability Stats

  • (Base 80 + 2 Armor per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Aura: Increases Magic Armor of all allies by 200

Antimatter Sphere - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

Dr. Zeno uses antimatter to create a protective sphere around him when he is close to death. The Antimatter Sphere can deflect a single attack of any type or strength, and also restores a small amount of Health

Ability Stats

  • (Base 4800 + 80 Health per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Restores 8000 Health after blocking attack
