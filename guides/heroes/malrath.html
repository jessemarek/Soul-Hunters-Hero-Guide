Malrath - Mutant Beast


"You know not what you have summoned!"


"You know not what you have summoned!"

Abilities

Blood Harvest - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Malrath harvests the blood of all enemy units on the battlefield, dealing Physical Damage and putting them in a weakened state. In their weakened state, they will have reduced Lifesteal, and will receive unblockable Physical Damage every time they move (or are knocked back)

Ability Stats

  • (+ 18 Damage, 2% Damage and 0.8% Lifesteal per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1800 Damage
    Enemy Takes 200% Damage based on distance covered
    Enemy Lifesteal reduced by 80%
    Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be affected every time

Scavenger - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

When an enemy unit dies on the battlefield, Malrath is able to gain a portion of the unit's Max Health. If the unit was killed by Malrath, the effect is doubled

Ability Stats

  • (Base 10.1 + 0.2% Max Health and Base 5 + 0.01% Max Health Summoned per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: When hero dies, gains 30.1% of their Max Health
    When summoned unit dies, gains 15% of their Max Health

Thirst for Blood - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Malrath ambushes the enemy, dealing Physical Damage that cannot be blocked and interrupting any abilities the enemy is using. Some of the Damage dealt by this attack will be returned to Malrath as Health

Ability Stats

  • (Base 200 + 10 Damage and Base 12.85 + 0.15% Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 1000 Damage
    24.85% of the Damage dealt by ability is transferred to Malrath as Health

Relentless Assault - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Malrath can detect the Health levels of all enemy heroes on the battlefield. When an enemy has low Health, Malrath will go into rage and continuously attack that enemy until they die or Malrath dies. While in rage, Malrath's Physical Attack and Attack Speed are increased, but he also takes 30% more Damage

Ability Stats

  • (Base 16 + 0.4% Attack Speed, Base 342 + 8 Physical Attack and Base 40 + 0.1% Additional Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Increase Attack Speed by 40%
    Increase Physical Attack by 822
    Addition Damage taken reduced by 10%

Flowing Blood - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

Malrath's "Blood Harvest" affects female heroes for a longer period, and the Lifesteal effect of "Thirst for Blood" is increased against female heroes

Ability Stats

  • (Base 59.8 + 1% Time and Base 30 + 0.5% Health per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Effective time of "Blood Harvest" lengthened by 99.8%
    Health gained from "Thirst for Blood" increased by 50%
