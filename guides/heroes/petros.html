Abilities

Volcano - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Petros tears up the ground in front of him, dealing Damage to enemies standing on it and knocking them out of position. It also turns the battlefield into a volcano for 20 seconds. While the battlefield is a volcano, Petros' Abilities and Basic Attacks will be stronger than usual. If Petros' "Burning Coals" Ability is unlocked, burning coals will immediately come out of the volcano, the amount depending on Petros' Star-Level

Ability Stats

  • (+ 25 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 2500 Damage
    Lift Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Lifted every time

Earth Smash - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Petros furiously smashes the ground around him, dealing AoE Physical Damage and causing the ground to collapse. If enemies stand on the collapsed ground for too long, lava will shoot up from the earth, knocking them into the air and dealing Damage. When the battlefield is a volcano, the Damage dealt by the shooting lava will be greater

Ability Stats

  • (+ 15 Base Damage and + 15 Lava Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1500 Damage
    Shooting lava deals a base of 1500 Damage
    Lift Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Lifted every time
  • Attributes:

Burning Coals - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Petros spits out burning coals, which explode when they take a certain amount of Damage, dealing AoE Physical Damage. The sooner they explode, the more Damage they deal. The coals will explode immediately if they are struck by one of Petros' own Abilities or by another exploding coal. While the battlefield is a volcano, their explosions deal more Damage and affect a wider area, and the coals will be produced whenever one of Petros' other abilities is used

Ability Stats

  • (Base 1000 + 50 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Exploding coals deal a base of 5000 Damage
  • Attributes:

Fire Spirit - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Petros receives the blessing of the fire spirit. This increases his Physical Attack, Physical Armor, and Magic Armor, and he becomes immune to Control Abilities when his Health is close to full. If an enemy manages to kill Petros, the fire spirit will bless the enemy who kills him, meaning that when the enemy takes an attack that otherwise would have killed them, they will recover a large amount of Health instead

Ability Stats

  • (Base 20% + 0.5% per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Petros will lose immunity to Control Abilities after losing 50% of his Health

Lava Burst - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

The lava contained in Petros' body bursts out, dealing Damage to enemies that are attacking Petros from in front of him. The Damage dealt by this ability is greater once Petros has taken a certain amount of Damage

Ability Stats

  • (Base 900 + 15 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Deals a base of 1500 Deflected Damage
