Abilities

Spring Breeze - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Summon a spring breeze to restore Health of all allies

Ability Stats

  • (Base 99 + 11 Health per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Restore a base of 1199 Health per wave

Elven Charm - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Confuse a single target with a Charm spell, causing them to turn and Attack their own allies.

Ability Stats

  • (+ 1 Success Rate per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Charm Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Charmed every time

Dazzling Light - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Unleash a dazzling flash to Blind multiple enemies and greatly decrease their Physical Attack Hit Rating

Ability Stats

  • (Base 72 + 3.6 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 360 Damage
    Blind Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Blinded every time

Natural Protection - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Become one with nature and reduce Magic Damage suffered by all allies

Ability Stats

  • (Base 4 + 0.1% per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Aura: Reduce the amount of Magic Damage your allies receive by 10%
