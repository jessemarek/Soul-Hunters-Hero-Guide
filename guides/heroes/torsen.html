Abilities

Gold Skin - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Transform into giant dwarf with hardened gold dwarven armor. Increase Attack, Health and Dodge, while reducing Physical Damage taken

Ability Stats

  • (Base 90 + 10 Physical Armor and Base 1 + 1 Dodge per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Increase Physical Armor by x for 1090 seconds
    Increase Dodge by 101, Physical Attack by 20%, Max Health by 20% and reduce Physical Damage taken by 40%

Earthquake - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Smash the ground with tremendous force, dealing AoE Physical Damage and Slowing enemies

Ability Stats

  • (+ 12 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1200 Damage
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time

Anvil Drop - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Call down a giant mace from the sky on an enemy's head, dealing Physical Damage

Ability Stats

  • (Base 320 + 16 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 1600 Damage
    Blind Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Blinded every time
