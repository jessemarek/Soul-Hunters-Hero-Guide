Abilities

Nature's Fury - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Dokras summons a lightning storm which successively strikes each enemy in random order, dealing Magic Damage. Each strike deals 50% more damage than the previous

Ability Stats

  • (+ 15 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: The first strike deals 1500 Damage

Rise of the Warriors - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Dokras summons two tiny but fearsome hedgehog warriors, which possess strong Magic Armor

Ability Stats

  • (+ Level per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Summons two level 100 Hedgehog Warriors

Entangle - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

An enemy becomes tangled in vines, unable to move, Dodge, or use Physical Attacks

Ability Stats

  • (Base 20 + 1 Success Rate per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Entangle Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Entangled every time

Spirits' Protection - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Dokras asks the spirits for protection, reducing the amount of Magic Damage he receives

Ability Stats

  • (Base 12 + 0.3% per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Passive: Reduces Magic Damage taken by 30%

Lying in Wait - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

At the start of battle, Dokras will be held in reserve off the battlefield, over time building up Physical Attack, Attack Speed and Energy. He may be called into battle at any time. When fighting in the Arena, he will enter battle 30 seconds after the battle begins

Ability Stats

  • (Base 120 + 2 Physical Attack and Base 2 + 0.1 Attack Speed per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Increase Physical Attack by 200 every second
    Increase Attack Speed by 6 every second
