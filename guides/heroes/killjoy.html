Abilities

Doppleganger - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Killjoy releases a forcefield which creates an evil doppelganger of enemies it touches. The doppelganger attack their enemy counterparts with the Basic Attack of that enemy. While the ability is in effect, if the doppelganger is killed, another one will take its place. A doppelganger will not appear for bosses or summoned units

Ability Stats

  • (+ 1 Level per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Create Dopplegangers of enemies up to level 100

Elemental Sphere - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Killjoy summons a ring of energy spheres which rotate around him, dealing Magic Damage-over-Time to nearby enemies. The ability can be applied again when already in use, doubling the effect

Ability Stats

  • (+ 7 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 700 Damage per second

Puppet Master - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Killjoy uses magic to take a Front Line enemy and push them back, bringing a Mid or Back Line enemy to the Front. This ability also deals a large amount of Magic Damage to the Front Line enemy

Ability Stats

  • (Base 300 + 15 per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 1500 Damage
    Knock-back Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Knocked back every time

Taunt - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Killjoy's taunts enrage an enemy, lowering their Magic Armor, but increasing their Physical Attack

Ability Stats

  • (Base 80 + 2 Physical Attack and Base 160 + 4 Magic Armor per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Increases Physical Attack by 200
    Lowers Magic Armor by 400
    Taunt Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be affected every time

Doppleganger Bonus - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

When Killjoy uses his Ultimate, doppelgangers of both friendly and enemy heroes will form. Whenever a doppelganger is formed, Killjoy will recover some Health

Ability Stats

  • (Base 630 + 10 Health per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Restore a base of 1030 Health per wave
