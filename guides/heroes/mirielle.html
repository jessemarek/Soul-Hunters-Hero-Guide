Abilities

Shadow Warrior - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Mirielle summons Shadow Warriors that appear in front of every enemy hero. Identical in appearance to Mirielle, these warriors also have Mirielle's "Lone Assassin" ability if it is unlocked. Clicking on Mirielle's avatar again after her Ultimate has been activated allows Mirielle herself to replace the shadow warrior in front of the weakest enemy hero

Ability Stats

  • (+ 1 Level per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Summons level 100 Shadow Warrior

Mirielle's Touch - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

With the touch of her hand, Mirielle can shatter enemy Physical Armor and deal AoE Physical Damage that ignores enemy Armor. Enemies affected by Mirielle's Touch will also have their Attack and Movement Speed slowed. At the same time, Mirielle's own Movement and Attack Speed will be increased

Ability Stats

  • (+ 6 Damage and 0.1% Attack and Movement Speed per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 600 Damage
    Reduces Attack and Movement Speed by 10%
    Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be affected every time

Deflection Stone - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Blocks a certain amount of Damage and deflects it at nearby enemies. Incoming attacks that ignore Physical Armor cannot be blocked by this ability. Damage done by the deflection ignores enemy Physical Armor

Ability Stats

  • (Base 2 + 0.1% Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Blocks 10% of incoming Damage

Lone Assassin - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Mirielle's Basic Attack ignores enemy Physical Armor. When there are fewer than 10 heroes remaining on the battlefield, her Basic Attack will also deal additional Damage. The fewer heroes there are, the more Damage her Basic Attack deals

Ability Stats

  • (Base 170 + 3 Damage and Base 597 + 15 Physical Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Damage increases by 350 for every hero fewer than 10 on the Battlefield
    Deals a base of 1497 Physical Damage that ignores enemy Armor

Energy Drain - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

After activating "Shadow Warrior", the first three Basic Attacks delivered by Mirielle and each of her Shadow Warriors will have a Energy draining effect

Ability Stats

  • (Base 60 + 1 Energy per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Each Attack drains 100 Energy
