Enrique - Charming Virtuoso


"You and I will defeat this creature together!"


"You and I will defeat this creature together!"

Hero Info and Role

Middle Line Support: Enrique's Buffs can help his party Attack more quickly and Dodge more effectively. He is adept at disrupting the enemy party with an AoE Hypnotize and Blind

Abilities

Hypnotic Lullaby - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

With a few plucks of his harp, Enrique can Hypnotize the entire enemy party, leaving them vulnerable to further attacks

Ability Stats

  • (+ 1 Success Rate per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Hypnosis Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Hypnotized every time

Vigorous Melody - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Playing an upbeat tune increases the Attack Speed of one of your allies

Ability Stats

  • (Base 19 + 1 Attack Speed per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Increase Attack Speed by 119 for 6 seconds

Ominous Serenade - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

An enchanting melody not only deals AoE Magic Damage, but also Stuns enemies within range

Ability Stats

  • (Base 160 + 8 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 800 Damage
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time

Heroic Ode - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Enrique plays an ode to the ancient heroes, granting his whole party an increased Dodge Rating

Ability Stats

  • (Base 20 + 0.5 Dodge per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Aura: Increase Dodge Rating of all allies by 50

Song of Healing - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

After Enrique uses "Hypnotic Lullaby", nearby allies will restore some Health over an extended period

Ability Stats

  • (Base 251 + 4 Health per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Allies restore 411 Health per second for 20 seconds
