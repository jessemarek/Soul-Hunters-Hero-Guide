Abilities

Strategic Transformation - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

This Ultimate causes Kasar to switch between two modes (statue and flying), and does not use up Energy. As he transforms into flying mode, enemies are terrified, causing them to have reduced Movement and Attack Speed. While in flying mode, he has increased Magic Attack is able to Attack the enemy, but all Attacks as well as the act of flying expend Energy. As he transforms into statue mode, nearby enemies take Magic Damage and are Stunned. While in statue mode, he is unable to actively attack, but he gradually gains Health and Energy and is immune to 40% of the Damage he receives

Ability Stats

  • (+ 12 AoE Damage, Base 20 + 25 Health Recovered and + 24 Magic Attack per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals 1200 AoE Damage after transforming into statue form
    Restores 2520 Health per second in statue form
    Magic Attack increases by 2400 in flying form
    Stun and Terrify Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be affected every time

Magic Overload - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

When Kasar transforms into statue mode, he gets a shield that can resist Damage. When he transforms into flying mode, he immediately ambushes the weakest enemy

Ability Stats

  • (+ 30 Damage and Base -30 + 90 Shield HP per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Ambush deals 3000 Damage
    Get shield with 8970 HP when switching to statue mode

Fatal Plunge - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

While in flying mode, Kasar picks up an enemy and carries them away. A few seconds later, the enemy falls from the sky, their impact with the ground dealing AoE Magic Damage and Stunning them, as well as Stunning surrounding enemies

Ability Stats

  • (Base 300 + 15 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 1500 Damage
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time

Specter of Death - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

While in flying mode, Kasar attacks an enemy who is currently affected by a Control Ability. A swarm of bats surrounds the enemy, dealing Magic Damage and extending the time that they are affected by the Control Ability

Ability Stats

  • (Base 800 + 20 Damage and Base 99.5 + 0.5% Time of Effect per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Deals a base of 2000 Damage
    Extends time of control effect by 129.5%

Vital Energy - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

When Kasar is in statue mode, whenever allied or enemy non-summoned units take Magic Damage, Kasar will gain some Energy. The amount of Energy recovered is proportional to the amount of Damage taken. As Kasar gains Energy, his Attacks gradually become less affected by enemy Magic Armor. His attacks become more effective in this way up to 2500 Energy

Ability Stats

  • (Base 277.5 + 4.5 Magic Armor per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Igonores a maximum of 457.5 Magic Armor
