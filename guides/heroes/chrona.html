Abilities

Time Reversal - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Chrona uses her mysterious powers to rewind time, sending her to all the locations on the battlefield she had previously visited during the current wave. Enemies she hits on her reverse journey will take Physical Damage. Lightning Beetles will also appear in various locations, these bugs will attack weak enemies when the Ultimate ends. Activating this Ultimate also dispels most Control Abilities or Curses placed upon Chrona

Ability Stats

  • (+ 9 Damage Enroute and + 10 Beetle Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals 900 Damage enroute
    Beetles deal 1000 Damage

Stealth Mode - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Chrona enters stealth mode and rapidly moves around the battlefield, dealing Physical Damage to enemies she hits. After using this ability Chrona will have increased Movement and Attack Speed for a short period

Ability Stats

  • (+ 8 Damage Enroute per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: 800 Damage enroute

Instant Rewind - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Chrona rewinds time to a moment in the past. She regains the amount of Health she had at that moment, and dispells most Control Abilities and Curses placed on her. She can use this ability a maximum of 2 times per wave. After using this ability, Chrona will get a permanent boost to her Physical Attack. (If Chrona takes Mortal Damage, and she still has a remaining use of this ability, she will automatically use it to rewind time to a time shortly before the mortal blow. She may only use this ability to save herself once per wave)

Ability Stats

  • (Base 100 + 5 Physical Attack per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Increases Physical Attack by 500 after rewind

Insect Swarm - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Chrona scatters biting flies all over the battlefield, which overwhelm enemies, reducing their Physical Armor. The flies will also expose the location of hidden or invisible enemies

Ability Stats

  • (Base 137 + 3 Physical Armor per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Reduces target's Physical Armor by 317
