Abilities

Spirit Swarm - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Sylphi summons a swarm of spirits to attack the enemy party, dealing Magic Damage. The spirits deal 150% damage to enemy summoned or illusionary units. A portion of the Damage dealt will be returned to Sylphi as Health once the spirits disperse

Ability Stats

  • (+ 8 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Each attack deals a base of 800 Damage

Flapping Wings - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Sylphi is assisted by a flock of bats which attack enemies over a large range, dealing Magic Damage. The bats deal 150% damage to enemy summoned or illusionary units

Ability Stats

  • (+ 15 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1500 Damage

Silence - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Sylphi uses dark magic to Silence enemies within a large range, temporarily limiting affected enemies to Physical Attacks

Ability Stats

  • (Base 20 + 1 Success Rate per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Silence Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Silenced every time

Unbreakable Resolve - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Sylphi's sorcery skills improve under pressure. Whenever she takes Damage equivalent to 20% of her Max Health, her Magic and Physical Armor are Buffed

Ability Stats

  • (Base 160 + 4 Magic Armor and Base 160 + 4 Physical Armor per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Increase Magic Armor by 400 each time
    Increase Physical Armor by 400 each time

Sorceress' Medallion - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

Heroes owning a Sorceress' Medallion will have their Magic Pierce Rating buffed while Awakened Sylphi is on the field

Ability Stats

  • (Base 120 + 2 Magic Pierce per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Aura: All teammates with medallion increase Magic Pierce Rating by 200
