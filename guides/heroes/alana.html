Alana - Dark Assassin


"My poison errodes even the best of armors!"


"My poison errodes even the best of armors!"

Hero Info and Role

Middle Line DPS: Alana's deadly Ultimate wreaks havoc on the enemy's mid and back lines. Her Energy Shield grants her immunity to both Physical and Magic Damage, making her a hard target to kill

First Appeared

Original Hero

  • Additional Cards grant 30
  • Drops in Diamond Chest

Abilities

Ambush - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Ambush and assassinate an unfortunate enemy, deal a large amount of Physical Damage that ignores enemy Armor, also hit nearby enemies with Splash Damage

Ability Stats

  • (+ 25 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 2500 Damage

Energy Shield - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Every time the Dark Assassin kills an enemy, she gains an Energy Shield with 5 layers. Every layer can block an attack and give the Dark Assassin 80 energy. She is also immune to control spells, while using the Energy Shield

Ability Stats

  • (Base 50 + 50 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Each layer of the shield can block a maximum of 5050 Damage

Poison Dart - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Shoot a toxic dart to deal Physical Damage equivalent to a basic attack and reduce an enemy's Physical Armor

Ability Stats

  • (Base 60 + 3 Physical Armor per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Reduce target's Physical Armor by 300 for 6 seconds

Assassin's Instincts - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

An assassin’s instinct makes killing second nature. Increase the whole Party's physical Attack

Ability Stats

  • (Base 160 + 4 Physical Attack per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Aura: Increase Physical Attack of all allies by 400

Reflecting Sphere - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

Whenever Alana dodges an enemy attack, she will gain an Energy Shield layer (see 2nd ability). Initially this ability cannot be activated more than once within 3 second period, but the actual cooldown period depends on ability level

Ability Stats

  • (Base 0.6 + 0.01 per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Reduce time between adding layers by 1 second
