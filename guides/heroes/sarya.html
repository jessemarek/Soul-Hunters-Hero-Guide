Abilities

Heaven's Wrath - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Calls down several beams of light that scorch random enemies, interrupting abilities and dealing Magic Damage. Although the beams deal Magic Damage, their strength depends on Sarya's Physical Attack

Ability Stats

  • (+ 20 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals 2000 Damage
    Interrupt Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be interrupted every time

Burning Beam - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Calls down a single beam of light that scorches a random enemy, interrupting abilities and dealing Magic Damage. Although the beams deal Magic Damage, their strength depends on Sarya's Physical Attack

Ability Stats

  • (+ 20 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals 2000 Damage
    Interrupt Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be interrupted every time

Flying Knife - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Sarya's blade ricochets around the enemy party, dealing Physical Damage. The amount of damage dealt diminishes each time the blade strikes an enemy. The amount of time it ricochets depends on how many stars Sarya has

Ability Stats

  • (Base 300 + 15 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals 1500 initial Damage
    Number of ricochets equal to number of stars. At 7 stars will ricochet up to 8 times

War God's Blessing - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Sarya blesses her allies with a prayer to the war god. All allies increase their Physical Hit Bonus

Ability Stats

  • (Base 48 + 1.2 Physical Hit Bonus per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Aura: all allies increase Physical Hit Bonus by 120

Blood Moon - Unlocks with Night Raider Skin

Ability Description

While Sarya is using her Ultimate, Enemies will gain 80% less Energy due to Damage taken

Ability Info

Ability only available while Night Raider Skin is equipped
