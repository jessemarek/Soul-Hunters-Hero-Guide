Ariel - Audacious Fairy


"I'll fly by your side!"


"I'll fly by your side!"

Hero Info and Role

Middle Line DPS: Skilled at using AoE Magic Attacks to inflict Damage on the entire enemy party. At Purple Fusion she also gains a shield that absorbs all types of Damage

First Appeared

Sign-In Hero

  • Requires 30 to summon
  • Additional Cards grant 18
  • Drops in Gold Chest

Abilities

Flower Power - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Cast a powerful spell known only to fairies, dealing severe Magic Damage to all enemies on the field

Ability Stats

  • (+ 15 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1500 Damage

Radiant Orb - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Unleash a brilliant orb which deals Magic Damage to enemies in its path

Ability Stats

  • (+ 7 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 700 Damage

Pixie Dust - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Throw pixie dust at the enemy party, dealing AoE Magic Damage as well as having a Silencing effect

Ability Stats

  • (Base 100 + 5 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 500 Damage
    Silence Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Silenced every time

Perpetual Shield - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Summon a shield that protects Ariel from both Magic and Physical Damage. The shield disappears after absorbing one hit, but it regenerates every 12 seconds

Ability Stats

  • (Base 3520 + 80 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Absorbs up to 8320 Damage

Multi-Shield - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

Allows for Ariel's Perpetual Shield to have multiple layers, meaning that it may now absorb more than one hit before breaking. Each time that one of Ariel's other abilities deals Damage to an enemy, there is a chance that another layer will be added to her Perpetual Shield

Ability Stats

  • (Base 4.9 + 0.02% per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: 4.98% chance of additional layer when Ariel attacks an enemy
