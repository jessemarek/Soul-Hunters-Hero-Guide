Abilities

Scarab Curse - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Tut summons a scarab beetle that marks an enemy for destruction (select an enemy by tapping Tut's avatar again when the enemy's image appears). When auto-battle is activated, the beetles will mark the enemy unit with the highest Energy. The mark is not visible to the enemy party. If the marked enemy activates their Ultimate while the scarab is on the battlefield, they will be lifted and take Magic Damage. Then, the beetle will spray poison on nearby enemies. Any enemy struck by the poison who already has the Pharaoh's Plague will develop a secondary infection

Ability Stats

  • (+ 10 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1000 Damage

Paraoh's Plague - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Tut releases a plague on enemies in range, instantly dealing Magic Damage. Enemies in range that are not already infected will then take Damage-over-Time, while enemies that are already infected have a chance of developing a secondary infection. (There are three types of secondary infections, each with a different effect: lowering Physical Armor, lowering Magic Armor, lowering Energy Bonus.) When enemy units are close to each other, a secondary infection can pass from a unit with high Magic Armor to one with lower Magic Armor. Enemies infected this way may not be infected again with a secondary infection until a certain amount of time passes

Ability Stats

  • (+ 15 Damage, + 4 Damage per second, + 2 Physical Armor, + 2 Magic Armor and + 0.4% Energy Bonus per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1500 Damage
    Deals a base of 400 Damage per second
    Lowers Physical Armor by 200
    Lowers Magic Armor by 200
    Lowers Energy Bonus by 40%

Mummify - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Tut mummifies an enemy and encloses them in a casket for several seconds, and then summons a duplicate unit that is identical in appearance to the mummified unit. The mummified enemy will focus their attacks on the duplicate unit until it is destroyed. Duplicate forms of bosses will not be summoned

Ability Stats

  • (Base 20 + 1 Level per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Summon level 100 Duplicate Unit

Pyramid Shield - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Tut creates an energy shield between two pyramids. Enemies that get close to the shield will be Knocked back and have their Energy depleted

Ability Stats

  • (Base 20 + 0.5 Energy per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Enemy Unit loses 50 Energy

Pharaoh's Fury - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

If an enemy has been infected with all three types of secondary infections from Pharaoh's Plague, they will die immediately. Most abilities that are normally triggered upon the unit's death will not be activated

Ability Stats

  • (Base 60 + 1 Level per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Effective against enemies under level 100
