Abilities

Whirlwind - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Summon a whirlwind to lift all enemies in its path and deal Magic Damage

Ability Stats

  • (+ 18 per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1800 Damage
    Whirlwind Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be affected every time

Power of the Serpent - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Harness the power of ancient serpents to temporarily increase one ally's Physical Attack

Ability Stats

  • (+ 9 Physical Attack per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Increase Physical Attack by 900 for 12 seconds

Mystery of the Serpent - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Harness the energy of ancient serpents and temporarily increase one ally's Magic Attack, and allows them to gain Health in proportion to the Magic Damage they deal

Ability Stats

  • (Base 180 + 9 Magic Attack and Base 23.8% + 0.2% Damage Converted per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Increase Magic Attack by 900 for 12 seconds
    53.8% of Magic Damage dealt is converted to Health gained

Predator's Instinct - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Like a snake, keep a watchful eye on prey and increase allies' Physical Hit Bonus

Ability Stats

  • (Base 40 + 1 Hit Bonus per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Aura: Increase Physical Hit Bonus of all allies by 100

Persistent Storm - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

After reaching the edge of the screen, the whirlwind summoned by Sumiko's Ultimate, persists for a period of time and moves in random directions. Enemies hit by the whirlwind will be Knocked out of position and will take Magic Damage

Ability Stats

  • (Base 482 + 8 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Deals 802 Damage while whirlwind lasts
