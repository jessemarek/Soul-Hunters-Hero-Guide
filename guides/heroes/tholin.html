Tholin - Barbarian of the North


"Born in the wilderness, Raised by the wild!"


"Born in the wilderness, Raised by the wild!"

Hero Info and Role

Front Line Tank: A multi-purpose Tank, Tholin can Stun enemies, Buff allies, and Physical Damage. He can also summon woodland beasts to terrorize the enemy

First Appeared

Conjuring Stone

Hero Card

  • Requires 80 to summon
  • Additional Cards grant 30
  • Drops in Diamond Chest

Available In

Crucible Supply Wagon

Stat Growth Rates

Health 72 Health 129
Physical Attack 5.5 Physical Attack 9.9
Magic Attack 0 Magic Attack 0
Physical Armor 2.5 Physical Armor 4.5
Magic Armor 2.2 Magic Armor 3.9

Hero Medallions

None

Abilities

Barbaric Roar - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Tholin unleashes an intimidating roar, Stunning enemies within a small area and dealing Physical Damage

Ability Stats

  • (+ 19.2 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1920 Damage
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time

Beast Master - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Tholin calls upon two fierce wolverines to join him in battle. These wolverines spit venomous darts which deal Physical Damage-over-Time, as well as having a Slowing effect on the enemy

Ability Stats

  • (+ 1 Level and + 3 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Summons two level 100 Wolverines
    Each dart deals 300 Damage per second for 3 seconds

Whirling Axe - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Tholin throws his war-axe at the most distant enemy, dealing Physical Damage before the axe flies back to his hand. Enemies in the path will be struck twice

Ability Stats

  • (Base 256 + 12.8 per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Every hit deals a base of 1280 Damage

Warrior's Heart - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

A lifetime of training in the northern woods gives Tholin an advantage in the heat of battle, allowing him to temporarily increase his Attack Speed as well as that of his allies

Ability Stats

  • (Base 40 + 0.1% Attack Speed per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Attack Speed increases by 10% for 5 seconds

Power of the Beast - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

Every time Tholin's wolverines attack the enemy, a random teammate will receive a boost in Energy

Ability Stats

  • (Base 53.6 + 0.4 Energy per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Teammate receives 69.6 Energy when Wolverines attack
