Abilities

Fearless Charge - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Tareth knocks an enemy into the air, then pummels them with a series of Physical Attacks. If the enemy dies during the course of her Ultimate, Tareth gets a large boost in Physical Attack

Ability Stats

  • (+ 15 Damage and + 20 Physical Attack per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1500 Damage
    Physical Attack increases by 2000 when enemy is killed by Ultimate

Arrowstorm - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Tareth unleashes a hail of flaming arrows, dealing AoE Physical Damage. The more enemies are struck by arrows, the more Tareth's allies receive a boost to Attack Speed and Movement Speed

Ability Stats

  • (+ 10 Damage and + 0.06% Movement and Attack Speed per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1000 Damage
    Every enemy struck increases Movement and Attack Speed by 6%
  • Attributes:

Knight's Protection - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Tareth increases the Lifesteal rating of a teammate or herself, as well as restoring some of their Health

Ability Stats

  • (Base 103 + 5 Health and Base 61 + 3 Lifesteal per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Restores 503 Health per second for 8 seconds
    Lifesteal rating increased by 301

Divine Courage - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Tareth's teammates are encouraged by her fearlessness and gain an increase in Physical Armor

Ability Stats

  • (Base 80 + 2 Physical Armor per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Aura: All allies' Physical Armor increased by 200
