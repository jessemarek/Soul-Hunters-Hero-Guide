Abilities

Earth Shatter - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Create a swath of destruction, dealing Physical Damage and Stunning enemies in its path

Ability Stats

  • (+ 14.4 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1440 Damage
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time

Thrill of the Hunt - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Leap into action, improving all allies' Physical and Magic Attack as well as Attack Speed

Ability Stats

  • (+ 5 Physical Attack and + 5 Magic Attack per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: All allies increase Physical Attack by 500 for 5 seconds
    All allies increase Magic Attack by 500 for 5 seconds

Chilling Howl - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Unleash a ghostly howl, dealing AoE Physical Damage and reducing enemy Armor

Ability Stats

  • (Base 108 + 5.4 Damage and Base 16 + 0.8 Physical Armor per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 540 Damage
    Reduce target's Physical Armor by 80

Prowess of the Wolf - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Grant your allies the prowess of a pack of wolves, increasing their Lifesteal

Ability Stats

  • (Base 21 + 0.5 Lifesteal per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Aura: Increase Lifesteal of all allies by 51
