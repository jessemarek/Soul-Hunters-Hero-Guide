Abilities

Blood Sacrifice - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Deadly blades rain down from the sky dealing Magic Damage to up to 5 enemies, every enemy hit returns a blood orb back to you. You will gain as much Health as the enemy loses

Ability Stats

  • (+ 21 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 2100 Damage

Dreadful Imprisonment - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Imprison one enemy and Stun them. If the enemy dies during the imprisonment they will transform into a bat. Every time the bat takes Damage it will Heal Vincent

Ability Stats

  • (+ 1 Success Rate per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Imprisonment Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Imprisoned every time

Blade Dance - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Perform a quick two hit sword combo, dealing AoE Magic Damage. Vincent will gain Health proportionate to the amount of Damage dealt

Ability Stats

  • (Base 280 + 14 Damage and Base 61.65% + 0.35% Health per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Every hit deals a base of 500 Damage
    Recovers Health equivalent to 89.65% of Damage dealt

Bloody Cloak - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Wear a blood cloak, increasing Magic Armor

Ability Stats

  • (Base 400 + 10 Magic Armor per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Passive: Increase Magic Armor by 1000

Vampire's Touch - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

Enemies struck by any of Vincent's attacks will be poisoned, meaning they have reduced Lifesteal and Healing Spells will be less effective on them. If they die from the poison, they will transform into a bat similar to that of "Dreadful Imprisonment". However, the bat will be relatively weak

Ability Stats

  • (Base 59.5 + 0.5% per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Enemy Lifesteal and Healing reduced by 95.5%
    Poison Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Poisoned every time
