Abilities

Thunderous Axe - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Deal a massive axe strike to a nearby enemy. If the enemy has less than 50% of his Health remaining, this Attack cannot be Dodged and will deal Double Damage

Ability Stats

  • (+ 22 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 2200 Damage
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time

Boomerang Blade - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Throw an axe which inflicts Physical Damage on every enemy in its path

Ability Stats

  • (+ 9.6 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Every hit deals a base of 960 Damage

Warcry - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Release a mighty roar to weaken the enemy's Physical Armor and deal AoE Physical Damage

Ability Stats

  • (Base 96 + 4.8 Damage and Base 20 + 1 Physical Armor per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 480 Damage
    Reduce target's Physical Armor by 100 for 5 seconds

Defensive Stance - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Move into a defensive stance to strengthen your Physical Armor

Ability Stats

  • (Base 20 + 0.5% per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Passive: Reduce Physical Damage received by 50%
