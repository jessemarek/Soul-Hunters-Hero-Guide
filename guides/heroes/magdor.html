Abilities

Dragon Heart - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Whenever Magdor has over 80% Energy, he can take the form of a dragon. In dragon form, his Basic Attack will deal Damage over a small range and Damage-over-Time. All his other abilities will become more powerful as well. However, Magdor's Energy is rapidly depleted while he is in dragon form

Ability Stats

  • (+ 3 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Basic Attack deals 300 Damage per second for 4 seconds

Dragon Fire - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Magdor blasts the enemy with dragonfire, dealing AoE Physical Damage which ignores enemy Armor

Ability Stats

  • (+ 8 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 800 Damage
  • Attributes:

Dragon Bash - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Magdor deals a blow to a single enemy with his shield, dealing Physical Damage as well as Knocking back and Stunning them

Ability Stats

  • (Base 240 + 12 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 1200 Damage
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time

Dragon Blood - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

The blood of a dragon flows through Magdor's veins, making him less vulnerable to Physical Attacks

Ability Stats

  • (Base 320 + 8 Physical Armor per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Passive: Increase Physical Armor by 800
