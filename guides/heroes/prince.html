Abilities

Sandstorm - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

The Prince will periodically unleash the Sands of Time, which will affect nearby enemies. Enemies affected by the sands will be Frozen in time and take Magic Damage. While in this state, if they take Damage equivalent to 70% of their current Health, they will die instantly (without being able to trigger abilities that revive them). When Frozen enemies take Damage or are killed, The Prince will gain Energy

Ability Stats

  • (+ 7 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 693 Damage
    Freeze Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Frozen every time

Combo Strike - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Depending on his position on the battlefield (e.g. near the edge, surrounded by enemies), The Prince will unleash a combo of attacks which are ideal for his current situation. The different series of attacks have different effects, but each combo ends with the same slashing attack which Freezes in time all enemies that it hits

Ability Stats

  • (+ 10 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Final attack deals 1000 Damage
    Freeze Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Frozen every time

Flanking Attack - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

The Prince retreats into the background, then flanks the enemy party and attacks the weakest enemy unit. The attack depletes 15% of the victim's Max Health and is unaffected by Armor. If the target is already Frozen in time, the attack will deal Damage equal to 25% of the target's Max Health

Ability Stats

  • (Base 4000 + 200 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a maximum of 20000 Damage

Tuck and Roll - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

The Prince often rolls to rapidly change his position and escape danger. While rolling, the Prince's Dodge Rating will be greatly increased. With each roll he completes, the Damage done by "Combo Strike" also increases. When battling manually, you may tap any location on the battlefield to control the direction of the roll. When auto-battle is activated, the direction will be determined automatically

Ability Stats

  • (Base 158.5 + 1.5 Dodge and Base 322 + 8 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Dodge Rating increases by 248.5 while rolling
    Next Combo Strike will deal 802 more Damage

Dark Prince - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

Over the course of the battle, the Prince is gradually influenced by dark forces. This process is accelerated when female teammates or the Prince himself take Damage. When the darkness reaches a certain threshold, the Prince will transform into "The Dark Prince", and remains in that state until the end of the wave. The Dark Prince loses a small amount of Health every second, but gains large increases to his Attack, Lifesteal, and Speed. A new series of attacks also becomes available to him in "Combo Strike"

Ability Stats

  • (Base 1525 + 25 Physical Attack per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Increase Physical Attack by 2525
