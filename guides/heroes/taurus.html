Abilities

Raging Bull - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Taurus' protector spirit takes the form of a bull and charges into battle. Taurus and his protector unleash a series of attacks on the enemy Front Line, dealing Magic Damage and Stunning enemies

Ability Stats

  • (+ 18 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1800 Damage
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time

Warrior Spirit - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Taurus calls upon his protector spirit to attack the enemy party, dealing Magic Damage

Ability Stats

  • (+ 15 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1500 Damage

Hammer of Judgement - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Taurus and his protector spirit simultaneously unleash this ability. The protector spirit's attack leaves enemies within range Stunned and deals Magic Damage. Meanwhile Taurus causes his teammates within range to recover some Energy

Ability Stats

  • (Base 240 + 12 Damage and Base 10 + 0.5 Energy per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 1200 Damage
    Restores 50 Energy
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time

Strategic Decision - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Taurus Buffs his teammates within range, based on which heroes are on the battlefield. If there are more Front Line heroes, all allied heroes in range will have their Physical Armor Buffed. If there are more Middle Line heroes, affected heroes will have their Physical and Magic Attacks boosted. Otherwise, allied heroes in range will be affected by Control Abilities for a shorter time

Ability Stats

  • (Base 160 + 4 Physical Armor, Base 320 + 8 Physical and Magic Attack and Base 12 + 0.3% per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Increase Physical Armor by 400
    Increase Physical and Magic Attack by 800
    Reduce time affected by Control Abilities by 30%
