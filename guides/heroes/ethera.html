"I didn't come light-years just to sit around!"

Hero Info and Role

Middle Line DPS: Ethera uses advanced alien technology to Freeze enemies in space and time, making them easy targets for her attacks and those of her allies

Abilities

Event Horizon - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Ethera creates a rip in space-time that covers the enemy formation. All enemies within range may be Frozen, but Ethera can move freely, repeatedly striking her helpless foes and dealing Physical Damage

Ability Stats

  • (+ 6 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Freeze Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Frozen every time
    Every hit deals a base of 600 Damage

Paralyze - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Ethera strikes enemies with her hammer made from an exotic alloy, dealing AoE Physical Damage and temporarily Freezing enemies them within a small area. If she is not within striking distance of enemies she will leap to the Front Lines to deliver this Attack, then return to her original position

Ability Stats

  • (+ 8 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 800 Damage
    Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be affected every time

Dimensional Shift - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Ethera increases her Dodge Rating. In addition, every time she successfully Dodges, Ethera will recover some Energy

Ability Stats

  • (Base 28.5 + 1.5 Dodge Rating and Base 8 + 0.1 Energy per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Increase Dodge Rating by 148.5
    Gain 16 Energy every time you Dodge an attack

Time Warp - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Every time Ethera uses her Basic Attack, it has a 50% of creating a time warp. All enemies in the time warp will be temporarily Slowed

Ability Stats

  • (Base 20 + 0.4% per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Reduce Speed of enemies within range by 44%

Wormhole - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

Ethera has a 20% chance of avoiding any type of Damage below a certain limit. She will still take Damage that exceeds the limit

Ability Stats

  • (Base 6000 + 100 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Up to 10000 Damage can be avoided

Spacetime Jump - Unlocks with Galactic Explorer Skin

Ability Description

Everytime Ethera uses "Paralyze" and jumps to the Front Line, she will regain 40% of the Health she lost in the last 3 seconds. Note* Also appears to work when Ethera uses "Event Horizon"

Ability Info

Ability only available while Galactic Explorer Skin is equipped
