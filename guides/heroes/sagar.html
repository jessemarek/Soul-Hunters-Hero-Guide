Abilities

Dragon Shock - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Sagar summons a bolt of lightning in the form of a dragon to strike the enemy hero with the highest Physical Attack, dealing Magic Damage to the target as well as other nearby enemies. Enemies hit by this Attack will also have greatly reduced Magic and Physical Crit Rating for a short period

Ability Stats

  • (+ 15 Damage, + 3 Physical Crit and + 3 Magic Crit per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1500 Damage
    Reduces enemy Physical Crit Rating by 300
    Reduces enemy Magic Crit Rating by 300
  • Attributes:

Lightning Orb - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Sagar shoots an electric orb at the nearest enemy hero, pushing them back to where they entered the battlefield and making them unable to use any Ability (except Passive Abilities) for a short time. The orb will shock the enemy 3 times over a period of several seconds, each time dealing Magic Damage

Ability Stats

  • (+ 8 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Lightning Orb deals a base of 800 Damage
  • Attributes:

High Voltage - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Sagar gives one enemy a positive electric charge and gives another a negative charge. Enemies with opposite charges will smash together, causing them to be Stunned. If there is only one enemy on the field, he/she will not be Stunned. After a short time, the charged enemies will be shocked, taking Magic Damage

Ability Stats

  • (Base 160 + 8 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 800
    Stun Success Rate increses with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time
  • Attributes:

Electric Field - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Sagar generates an electric field that lasts for several seconds. Enemies within the filed will have reduced Magic Armor, Attack Speed, and Movement Speed

Ability Stats

  • (Base 20 + 0.5 Magic Armor per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Lowers Magic Armor of enemies in range by 50

Static Shock - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

After his awakening, Sagar's Basic Attack will deal AoE damage. Enemies struck by his Basic Attack also have a risk of receiving a "static shock", which lowers their Physical and Magic Armor

Ability Stats

  • (Base 900 + 15 Damage, Base 60 + 1 Physical Armor and Base 60 + 1 Magic Armor per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Basic Attack deals a base of 1500 Damage
    Static Shock reduces Physical Armor by 100
    Static Shock reduces Magic Armor by 100
