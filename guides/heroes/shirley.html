Shirley - Crazed Butcher


"Stand back and let the butcher work!"


"Stand back and let the butcher work"

Hero Info and Role

Front Line Tank: Shirley uses a hook and cleaver to pull enemies within range and then deliver deadly attacks. She also has significant Lifesteal ability

Abilities

Meat Hook - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Shirley uses a hook and chain to pull an enemy towards her and deal Physical Damage regardless of enemy Armor. Tap Shirley's icon after her hook has been thrown to latch on to an enemy, If Manic Chop is unlocked, it will be used immediately after this Ultimate

Ability Stats

  • (Base 150 + 25 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 2650 Damage

Mortal Mist - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

A strange mist engulfs Shirley, dealing Damage-over-Time to both Shirley and nearby enemies. The mist also boosts Shirley's Magic Armor

Ability Stats

  • (+ 12 Damage to Shirley, + 0.5% Reduced and + 10 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1198 Damage per second to Shirley
    Reduces Magic Damage taken by 49.5%
    Deals a base of 1000 Damage per second to enemies

Manic Chop - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Shirley uses her hook and cleaver in a vicious attack, Stunning an enemy and dealing Magic Damage. She gains as much Health as the enemy loses. Then she delivers a powerful kick, Knocking the enemy back

Ability Stats

  • (Base 100 + 5 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 500 Damage
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be stunned every time

Insanity - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Shirley's thirst for blood provides her with a big boost to her Max Health

Ability Stats

  • (Base 2000 + 50 Health per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Passive: Increase Health by 5000

Infectious Fog - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

When enemies are hit by "Manic Chop", they will be shrouded in an Infectious Fog, causing them and other enemies in the area to take Damage-over-Time

Ability Stats

  • (Base 480 + 8 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Deals a base of 800 Damage per second
