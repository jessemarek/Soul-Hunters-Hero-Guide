Abilities

Apparition - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Alice summons an apparition of her voodoo doll that appears in the sky and devastates the enemy party, dealing AoE Magic Damage. Enemies affected by Mark of Vengeance will also be stunned for 4 seconds

Ability Stats

  • (+ 15 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1500 Damage
    Stun success rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time

Laser Burn - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Alice's doll shoots destructive lasers out of its eyes, dealing AoE Magic Damage. Enemies affected by Mark of Vengeance will also take Damage-over-Time

Ability Stats

  • (+ 10 Damage and + 4 Damage per second per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1000 Damage
    Deals a base of 400 Damage per second for 6 seconds
  • Attributes:

Rabbit Decoy - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Alice throws a rabbit doll into the enemy party, which draws the attention and the attacks of enemies. When the rabbit is destroyed, it will explode, dealing Magic Damage to nearby enemies. Enemies affected by Mark of Vengeance will take additional Damage

Ability Stats

  • (Base 240 + 12 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 1200 Damage

Mark of Vengence - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Whenever Alice takes damage equivalent to 10% of her max health, a skull icon will appear over the head of the enemy, who did the most damage to Alice. Her other abilities will have an additional effect against enemies marked by this ability. The icon disappears once Alice deals damage to the unit, but Alice will gain a permanent boost in Magic Attack, Magic Armor and Physical Armor (up to 5 times)

Ability Stats

  • (Base 240 + 6 Magic Attack, Base 160 + 4 Physical Armor and Base 160 + 4 Magic Armor per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Magic Attack increased by 600
    Increase Physical Armor by 400
    Increase Magic Armor by 400

Eternal Mark - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

The "Mark of Vengence" will no longer disappear when Alice deals Damage to the enemy. In addition, marked enemies can be marked again, which causes Alice's abilities to be even more effective against them. Each time a new layer is added, Alice will regain Health at a faster rate
