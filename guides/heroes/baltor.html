Abilities

Beast Within - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Baltor channels his inner beast, increasing his Physical Attack for 16 seconds

Ability Stats

  • (+ 18 Physical Attack per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Increases Physical Attack by 1800 for 16 seconds

Free Throw - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Baltor throws an iron orb at a single enemy, Knocking them back and dealing Physical Damage

Ability Stats

  • (+ 20 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 2000 Damage
    Knock-back Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Knocked back every time

Penalty Kick - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Baltor kicks the iron orb at a single enemy, Stunning them and causing Physical Damage

Ability Stats

  • (Base 400 + 20 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 2000 Damage
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time

Chain Whip - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Baltor slings an iron orb through enemy lines and whips it back by its chain, dealing AoE Physical Damage

Ability Stats

  • (Base 600 + 15 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Deals a base of 1500 Damage

Proximity Blast - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

Baltor's "Free Throw" and "Penalty Kick" Abilities will now deal AoE Damage instead of Single-Target Damage

Ability Stats

  • (Base 1080 + 18 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Deals a base of 1800 Damage
