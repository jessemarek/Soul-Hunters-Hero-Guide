Abilities

Toxic Blast - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Cyana summons a huge poison blossom which explodes, dealing Magic Damage and Damage-over-Time to the entire enemy party. Increases the level of all poison pools on the field by one

Ability Stats

  • (+ 10 Damage and Base 5 + 5 Damage per second per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1000 Damage
    Deals a base of 505 Damage per second for 5 seconds

Lethal Defender - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Cyana summons a predatory plant which deals Physical Damage to nearby enemies. If the plant is destroyed, a poison pool will form on the ground, which deals Damage-over-Time to enemies in the area. If there is already a poison pool in that position when a Lethal Defender dies, the pool will be leveled up by one

Ability Stats

  • (+ 1 Level, + 3 Damage and + 1.5 Additional Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Summons a level 100 Defender
    Poison Pools deal a base of 300 Damage per second
    Poison Pools deal an additional 150 Damage per second when upgraded

Poison Lily - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

A poison lily will rise from one of the poison pools on the battlefield. The flower lowers the Physical Armor of nearby enemies, and cannot be targeted with Attacks, although it may take AoE Damage

Ability Stats

  • (Base 20 + 1 Level and Base 70 + 3.5 Physical Armor per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Summons a level 100 Flower
    Reduces Physical Armor by 350

Deadly Vapor - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Cyana constantly emits an invisible mist of poison, causing all enemies to take increased Damage-over-Time from other Attacks

Ability Stats

  • (Base 8 + 0.2% per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Aura: Enemies take an additional 20% Damage-over-Time

Lethal Poison - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

"Noxious Cloud" and "Lethal Defender's" Attacks can poison enemies, if an enemy is already poisoned it will increase Damage-over-Time

Ability Stats

  • (Base 1800 + 30 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Maximum Damage: 3000
