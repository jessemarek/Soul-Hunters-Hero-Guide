Abilities

Guardian Totem - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Summons a guardian totem that deals Damage to the enemy. With each attack, the guardian deals Magic Damage to a single target

Ability Stats

  • (Base 44 + 6 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Each attack deals 644 Damage

Art of Healing - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Radulf casts a spell which allows nearby allies to regain their strength, restoring some of their Health

Ability Stats

  • (Base 18 + 3 Health per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Restores a base of 318 Health per wave

Paralyze - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Radulf throws vials that contain a potent paralysis agent towards the enemy party. The vials bounce around among Radulf's enemies, and each time they hit an enemy, that enemy takes Magic Damage and may be Stunned. Summoned and illusionary units will be Stunned for a longer period, and take twice the Damage

Ability Stats

  • (Base 200 + 10 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 1000 Damage
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time

Ancient Curse - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Radulf uses an Ancient Curse to cripple enemies within a small area. Enemies affected by the Curse will take Damage-over-Time, and all Physical and Magic Damage they take from other attacks will be increased by 10%. The Curse lasts for 8 seconds

Ability Stats

  • (Base 200 + 5 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Deals a base of 500 Damage per second for 8 seconds
    Curse Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Cursed every time
