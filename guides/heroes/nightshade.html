Nightshade - Night Walker


"Let my blade do its work!"


"Let my blade do its work!"

Hero Info and Role

Middle Line Physical DPS: Energy Recovery is relatively fast, can rush the enemy Back Line and deal heavy Damage

First Appeared

Conjuring Stone

Hero Card

  • Requires 80 to summon
  • Additional Cards grant 30
  • Drops in Diamond Chest

Abilities

Cunning Flank - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Rush to the enemy's Back Line and switch to Melee Stance to attack the enemy. Her Melee Stance greatly increases her Attack Speed and Physical Damage. She will remain in the Back Line until the next wave or re-activating the Ultimate

Ability Stats

  • (Base 90 + 10 Attack per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Increase Attack by 1090 for 10 seconds

Shuriken Flurry - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Throws 3 deadly shurikens, dealing Physical Damage

Ability Stats

  • (+ 8 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Every hit deals a base of 800 Damage

Distortion - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Distort your appearance, increasing your Dodge Rating. When active you gain a small amount of Energy after a successful Dodge

Ability Stats

  • (Base 48.5 + 1.5 Dodge Rating and Base 19 + 0.2 Energy per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Increase Dodge Rating by 168.5 for 12 seconds
    Gain 35 Energyevery time you Dodge an attack

Deadly Precision - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Analyze the enemy's weaknesses, allowing you to land larger Critical Hits

Ability Stats

  • (Base 120% + 3% Crit Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Passive: Increase Physical Crit Damage by 300%

Battle Strategy - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

Nightshade increases her will to fight, adding an extra strike to Shuriken Flurry with higher Damage. For the first three hits, there is a chance to throw a dagger that Poisons or Stuns a target

Ability Stats

  • (Base 207 + 3 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Deal 327 Damage a second for a short duration
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time

Eagle's Instinct - Unlocks with Eagle Wings Skin

Ability Description

For every 2% Health an enemy has lost, Nightshade's Attack will be 1% more powerful

Ability Info

Ability only available while Eagle Wings Skin is equipped
