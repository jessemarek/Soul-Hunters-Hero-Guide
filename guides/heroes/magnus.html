Abilities

Sniper Threat - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Snipe the weakest enemy, dealing Physical Damage. This attack cannot be Dodged and is guaranteed to Crit. If the attack kills the target, Magnus will enter stealth mode, sneaking to a part of the battlefield more distant from his enemies, and restoring some of his Energy

Ability Stats

  • (+ 36 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 3600 Damage

Headshot - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Deals Physical Damage to a single enemy, as well as momentarily stunning them

Ability Stats

  • (+ 25 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 2500 Damage
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time

Grenade Launcher - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Launch a grenade which deals Physical Damage to several enemies and Slows them. The enemies will gradually recover their normal speed over time. This ability is used once at the beginning of every wave, as well as occasionally throughout the rest of the battle

Ability Stats

  • (Base 200 + 10 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 1000 Damage
    Slow Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Slowed every time

High Caliber - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

When there are no enemy heroes in the immediate vicinity of Magnus, his Physical Pierce Rating will increase, and his attacks will do additional Damage when they Crit

Ability Stats

  • (Base 300 + 7.5 Physical Pierce and Base 101.45 + 0.55 Crit Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Physical Pierce Rating increased by 750
    Crit deals an additional 134.45 Damage

Magic Bullets - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

Magnus is armed with a limited amount of special bullets. They will be used when Magnus uses his Headshot ability. These bullets will automatically prioritize enemies who are currently using an ability. They have a chance to interrupt that ability and deal additional Damage that ignores Armor. The amount of special bullets Magnus carries depends on his star-level

Ability Stats

  • (Base 7080 + 120 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Deals an Additional 11880 Damage that ignores Armor

Winter's Breath - Unlocks with Artic Warfare Skin

Ability Description

When Magnus kills an Enemy with his Ultimate, other Enemies suffer a morale collapse, resulting in a permanent 10% drop in Physical and Magic Attack. The effect can stack up to 3 times

Ability Info

Ability only available while Artic Warfare Skin is equipped
