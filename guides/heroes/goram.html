Abilities

Distorting Blow - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Goram swings his giant sword, delivering a blow so powerful that spacetime itself is distorted. Because of the distortion, Damage dealt is randomly distributed on all enemy units. When the attack is complete, a random enemy will take some aftershock Damage

Ability Stats

  • (Base 80 + 18 Damage and Base 80 + 18 Aftershock Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1880 Damage
    Deals a base of 1880 Aftershock Damage

Allies of Chaos - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Goram summons 3 illusionary versions of himself from another plane of existence to join the battle. Illusions warriors share Goram's 3rd and 5th (Awakening) ability if these abilities are unlocked

Ability Stats

  • (+ 1 Level per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Summons level 100 Illusion

Entropy - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Goram has more explosive power in his Attacks than any mortal being, thus increasing his Physical Crit Rating. Goram's other abilities will also have additional effects: whenever an attack hits an enemy, there is a chance that the enemy's Physical Armor will be reduced. If an attack Crits, it will deal a random amount of additional Crit Damage

Ability Stats

  • (Base 60 + 3 Physical Armor and Base 80 + 4 Crit Rating per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Decreases target's Physical Armor by 300
    Passive: Increase Physical Crit Rating by 400

Winds of Fate - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Goram unleashes an Attack at a random enemy which Stuns them for a random amount of time and deals a random amount of Damage (Damage dealt and Stun time are random within certain limits)

Ability Stats

  • (Base 480 + 12 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Deals a base of 1200 Damage
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time

Divine Shield - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

Goram' Attacks have a chance of collecting Magic fragments from the enemy, giving Goram a Magic Shield. Once he has the shield, collecting more fragments will further strengthen his shield so it can absorb more Damage

Ability Stats

  • (Base 2400 + 40 per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Each fragment allows the shield to absorb 4000 more Damage
