Abilities

Strafing Strike - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Namtar launches himself at a single enemy, dealing Physical Damage and lifting them into the air

Ability Stats

  • (+ 27 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 2700 Damage
    Lift Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Lifted every time

Demonic Slash - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Namtar descends upon an enemy, delivering a series of vicious slashing attacks which deal Physical Damage and may lift the enemy

Ability Stats

  • (+ 12.6 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1260 Damage
    Lift Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Lifted every time

Call of the Void - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Namtar's otherworldly howl sends shivers down the spine of his opponents, lowering their Physical and Magic Attack Stats

Ability Stats

  • (Base 220 + 10 Physical Attack and Base 220 + 10 Magic Attack per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Reduces target's Physical Attack by 1020
    Reduces target's Magic Attack by 1020

Escape into Darkness - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Namtar slips into the shadows, increasing his resistance to Magic Attacks

Ability Stats

  • (Base 320 + 8 per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Passive: Increase Magic Armor by 800

Persistent Threat - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

The longer Namtar survives in battle, the more his Physical Attack, Physical Armor, and Magic Armor will increase

Ability Stats

  • (Base 320 + 5 Physical Attack, Base 64 + 1 Physical Armor and Base 64 + 1 Magic Armor per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Every 10 seconds Physical Attack increases by 520
    Every 10 seconds Physical Armor increases by 104
    Every 10 seconds Magic Armor increases by 104
