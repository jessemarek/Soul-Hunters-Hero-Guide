Abilities

Nuclear Blast - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Lars detonates his nuclear device, dealing Physical Damage that ignores enemy Armor over a large range. Lars escapes the blast and parachutes to safety on the allied side of the battlefield before returning to battle

Ability Stats

  • (+ 17 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Explosion deals a base of 1700 Damage

Relocate - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

A tree appears on a random location on the battlefield, then Lars throws a hook and pulls himself to the location of the tree, dealing Physical Damage that ignores enemy Armor and Stunning enemies he encounters on the way

Ability Stats

  • (+ 6 per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 600 Damage
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time

Spinning Sawblade - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Lars throws a huge circular sawblade into the enemy party, which deals Physical Damage to enemies it hits on the way to its target, ignoring their Armor. When it reaches its target, it will spin in the air for a short period, dealing AoE Physical Damage that ignores Armor and Slowing affected enemies

Ability Stats

  • (Base 100 + 5 Damage Enroute and Base 40 + 2 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 500 Damage enroute
    Deals a base of 200 Damage at target location
    Slow Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Slowed every time

Dynamic Armor - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Whenever Lars receives a significant amount of ordinary Physical Damage, he will gain some Health and Physical Armor proportional to the amount of Damage taken. When he takes a significant amount of Magic Damage, he will gain some Health and Magic Armor. If he receives Physical Damage that ignores Armor, he will gain Health, Physical Armor, and Magic Armor

Ability Stats

  • (Base 21.5 + 0.5 Physical Armor, Base 21.5 + 0.5 Magic Armor and Base 133.5 + 2 Health per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Physical Armor increases by a base of 51.5
    Magic Armor increases by a base of 51.5
    Restores a base of 253.5 Health per second

Whirlwind 0f Death - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

Lars spins in place with his saw, momentarily Stunning nearby enemies, and dealing Physical Damage that ignores enemy Armor. If he strikes enemies who have less than 50% Health, their Health will be continuously drained unless they are Healed to the point that they have more than 50% Health remaining

Ability Stats

  • (Base 360 + 6 Damage and Base 2365 + 40 Health per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Deals a base of 600 Damage
    Drains 3965 Health per second
    Stun and Health Drain Success Rate increase with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be affected every time
