Vulko - Invisible Threat


"Unseen, unheard, like a shadow in the night!"


"Unseen, unheard, like a shadow in the night!"

Hero Info and Role

Front Line DPS: Vulko's ability to Attack while invisible puts fear into the hearts of his enemies

First Appeared

Conjuring Stone

Hero Card

  • Requires 80 to summon
  • Additional Cards grant 30

Available In

Not Released

Stat Growth Rates

Health 68 Health 123
Physical Attack 8.3 Physical Attack 15
Magic Attack 0 Magic Attack 0
Physical Armor 2.5 Physical Armor 4.5
Magic Armor 2.3 Magic Armor 4.2

Hero Medallions

None

Abilities

Art of the Kill - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Vulko moves to the edge of the battlefield, then Silences enemies within a certain area. Finally, he unleashes a series of long-distance Physical Attacks against an enemy

Ability Stats

  • (+ 16 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1600 Damage

Concealed Threat - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

For a short period, Vulko will become invisible. While invisible, he cannot be hit by enemy attacks and his Physical Attack Rating will increase

Ability Stats

  • (+ 16 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1600 additional Damage

Bloodbath - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Whenever Vulko attacks an enemy from behind, that enemy will take additional Physical Damage. The second time Vulko attacks that enemy from behind, a "Bloodbath" effect will be triggered, meaning even more Damage will be dealt and Vulko will increase his Physical Attack Rating

Ability Stats

  • (Base 380 + 19 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 1900 Damage

Deceptive Strike - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Vulko dashes behind the back of an enemy unit and then delivers a deadly Physical Attack

Ability Stats

  • (Base 680 + 17 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Deals a base of 1700 Damage

Obliterate - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

Whenever one of Vulko's attacks has a "Bloodbath" effect, Vulko will earn one point. Once he earns two points, he will unleash a devastating Physical Attack

Ability Stats

  • (Base 1260 + 21 per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Deals a base of 2100 Damage
