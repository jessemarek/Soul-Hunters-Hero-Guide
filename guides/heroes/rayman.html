Abilities

Power Gloves - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Rayman gets a pair of blue boxing gloves which immediately hit the enemies in front of Rayman, dealing Physical Damage. After getting these boxing gloves, his Basic Attacks and Ultimate will deal Additional Damage for the rest of the wave. If he uses his Ultimate again during the wave, the gloves will turn red, becoming more powerful and adding a Damage-over-Time effect which can stack

Ability Stats

  • (+ 17 Damage, + 9 Blue Glove Damage and + 20 Red Glove Damage-over-Time per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1700 Damage
    Blue Glove deals an additional 900 Damage
    Red Gloves deal 2000 Damage-overTime

Telescopic Fist - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Rayman winds up, then reaches further and punches harder than usual, dealing Damage and Knocking an enemy back (while winding up, he is immune to control abilities)

Ability Stats

  • (+ 33 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 3300 Damage

Leap and Smash - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Rayman steps on his enemies' heads several times, dealing Physical Damage and momentarily Stunning them. While using this ability he is immune to Control Abilities and has an increased Dodge Rating. The amount of times he can leap between enemies is equal to the amount of Stars he has. On his final leap, he will smash the ground, dealing Damage over a small area and Lifting nearby enemies. The Damage dealt by this final leap depends on the number of leaps he made previously

Ability Stats

  • (Base 100 + 5 Damage and Base 20.2 + 0.8 Dodge Rating per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 500 Damage
    Increase Dode Rating by 84.2 while leaping
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time

Extra Life - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

If Rayman steps on 7 enemies while using "Leap and Smash" over the course of a wave, Rayman will earn one heart. Each heart will protect Rayman from taking a fatal amount of Damage one time. Instead of dying he will gain some Health and be immortal for a short period. The hearts may protect Rayman a maximum of twice per wave

Ability Stats

  • (Base 18.9% + 0.1% Health Recovery per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Recovers 24.9% of Max Health

Morale Boost - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

Rayman reduces the amount of time nearby allied Heroes are affected by enemy Control Abilities

Ability Stats

  • (Base 1.79 + 0.03 Seconds per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Reduce time affected by Control Abilities by 2.99 seconds
