"Conquer and Enslave!"


"Conquer and Enslave!"

Abilities

Dark Sacrifice - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Havoc picks the weakest enemy as the victim of his sacrifice to the gods, inflicting a large amount of Magic Damage. If the enemy dies from this attack, Havoc will receive a boost to his Physical Attack, Physical Armor, Magic Attack, and Magic Armor. Otherwise, Havoc will sacrifice the weakest of his teammates, if the teammate dies Havoc will get twice the boost in Attack and Armor

Ability Stats

  • (+ 35 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 3500 Damage

Pandora's Box - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Instead of using his Basic Attack, Havoc releases from his box a rain of flames which falls upon his enemies, dealing AoE Magic Damage

Ability Stats

  • (+ 5 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 500 Damage
  • Attributes:

Sorcerer's Staff - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Havoc's staff causes chaos in the enemy party, dealing Magic Damage to enemies within range and disarming them

Ability Stats

  • (Base 160 + 8 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 800 Damage
    Disarm Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under leve 100 will be Disamred every time

Underworld Curse - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Aura: Havoc releases a curse on his enemies, debuffing their Physical Attack

Ability Stats

  • (Base 20.85 + 0.15% per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Aura: Lowers Physical Attack of all enemies by 29.85%

Final Gift - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

When Havoc dies, the boost in Attack and Armor he earned from using "Dark Sacrifice" will be transferred to a random teammate. The teammate will also receive some Health and Energy

Ability Stats

  • (Base 1800 + 30 and Base 90 + 1.5 Energy per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Restores 3000 Health
    Restores 150 Energy
