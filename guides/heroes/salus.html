Abilities

Gods' Strength - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Salus receives the strength of the war god, giving a large temporary boost to his Physical Attack and Attack Speed. But during this time period, he will only be able to use his "Swinging Blade" Ability. While this Ultimate is effective, enemy Control Abilities will affect Salus for a much shorter period than usual. This benefit gradually wears off

Ability Stats

  • (+ 60 Physical Attack per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Physical Attack increases by 6000 while Ultimate is effective

Storm Hammer - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Salus throws a huge hammer at an enemy. The target enemy will be Stunned, while both the target and other nearby enemies will take Damage

Ability Stats

  • (+ 20 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 2000 Damage
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time
  • Attributes:

Swinging Blade - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Salus swings his huge blade, dealing Damage to a target as well as other enemies behind them

Ability Stats

  • (Base 33.35 + 0.5 Splash Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Attack deals 73.35 Splash Damage

Battle Cry - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Salus unleashes a battle cry, increasing his team's Physical Armor and Attack Speed

Ability Stats

  • (Base 320 + 8 Physical Armor and Base 16 + 0.4% Attack Speed per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Increase Physical Armor by 800
    Increase Attack Speed by 40%

Orc's Rage - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

Salus' entire party will have increased Physical Crit Rate while he is on the battlefield

Ability Stats

  • (Base 120 + 2 Physical Crit per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Aura: All allies increase Physical Crit Rating by 200

Pulse Hammer - Unlocks with Crystal Warrior Skin

Ability Description

The "Storm Hammer" Ability is replaced with "Pulse Hammer". It will Attack enemies with Physical Armor first. Enemies will be Stunned regardless of their Physical Armor and their Physical Armor will be reduced 50%. Each time you level up the "Storm Hammer" Ability, the maximum eliminated Physical Armor is increased by 130

Ability Info

Ability only available while Crystal Warrior Skin is equipped
