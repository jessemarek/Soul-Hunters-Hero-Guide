Abilities

Celestial Ray - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Attack a single enemy with a focused ray of moonlight, dealing high Magic Damage and interrupts enemy attacks

Ability Stats

  • (+ 27 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 2700 Damage

Cresent Burst - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Selene charges her staff with the power of the moon unleashing a powerful ball of light, dealing Magic Damage and Stunning the target

Ability Stats

  • (+ 9 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 900 Damage
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time

Lunar Orb - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

A large orb of moonlight blasts through enemy lines, dealing AoE Magic Damage

Ability Stats

  • (Base 252 + 12.6 per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 1260 Damage

Moon Child - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Harness the mystical power of the moon, decreasing enemies' Magic Armor

Ability Stats

  • (Base 60 + 1.5 Magic Armor per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Aura: Decrease Magic Armor of all enemies by 150

Guiding Light - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

Under the watchful eye of the moon goddess, Selene is able to restore a large amount of Energy every time she kills an enemy using "Celestial Ray"

Ability Stats

  • (Base 368 + 2 Energy per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Gain an extra 448 Energy
