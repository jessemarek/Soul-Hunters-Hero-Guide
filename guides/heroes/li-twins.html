Abilities

Separation - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

The twins separate from each other and fight as two units on the battlefield. When separated, the twins use their abilities more quickly than usual. When both units' Health is reduced to zero, they will re-merge and continue fighting with the Health they had remaining before they separated. This Ultimate may not be used when the twins are already separated

Ability Stats

  • (+ 1 Level per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Separated Twins are at level 100

Light and Darkness - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Using the power of arcane magic, the dark twin strikes an enemy multiple times, dealing Magic Damage and Knocking them back. At the same time the white twin Heals a teammate multiple times

Ability Stats

  • (+ 4 Damage and + 3 Health per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 400 Damage
    Knock-back Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Knocked back every time
    Restores a base of 297 Health

Dual Magic - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

The twins unleash an ability that deals Magic Damage and Damage-over-Time to an enemy, while simultaneously increasing the Attack Speed and restoring some Health of a teammate

Ability Stats

  • (Base 100 + 5 Damage, Base 72 + 3 Damage and Base 80 + 4 Health per second per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 500 Damage
    Deals a base of 312 Damage per second for 6 seconds
    Gains a base of 400 Health per second for 6 seconds

Test of Purity - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

The twins isolate the most powerful enemy, and curse them with reduced Physical and Magic Attack. At the same time, they target the most powerful teammate and bless them with increased Physical and Magic Attack

Ability Stats

  • (Base 120 + 3 Damage, Base 151.5 + 3.5 Physical and Magic Attack Reduction and Base 200 + 5 Physical and Magic Attack Increase per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Deals a base of 300 Damage
    Reduce Physical and Magic Attack by 361.5 for 6 seconds
    Increase Physical and Magic Attack by 500 for 6 seconds

Divine Talent - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

With their mastery of arcane magic, all of the twins' abilities become more effective, and every 4th time they use an ability, it will affect an additional enemy

Ability Stats

  • (Base 6.9 + 0.1% Ability Effectiveness per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Ability Effectiveness increased by 10.9%
