Abilities

Requiescat in Pace - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Ezio targets the weakest enemy, launching an Attack which deals a large amount of Physical Damage and cannot be Dodged. If he succeeds in killing the target, Ezio will throw a Smoke Bomb which Blinds nearby enemies, reducing their Hit Rate

Ability Stats

  • (+ 10 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1000 Damage
    Blind Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Blinded every time

Kill Streak - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Ezio strikes each enemy on the field at least once with a quick and deadly attack, dealing Physical Damage and Stunning them. While carrying out these attacks he is immune to all types of Damage

Ability Stats

  • (+ 10 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1000 Damage
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time

Legendary Stealth - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Using his renowned stealth, every 12 seconds Ezio is able to Dodge a Physical Attack by which he normally would have been hit. He will also use throwing knives, which deal initial Physical Damage and Damage-over-Time

Ability Stats

  • (Base 240 + 12 Damage and Base 60 + 3 Damage per second per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 1200 Damage
    Deals a base of 300 Damage per second for 6 seconds

Eagle Vision - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Ezio activates his sixth-sense, increasing his Dodge Rating. After successfully Dodging, he will use his Basic Attack and Kill Streak more quickly. He will also gain 50 Energy every time he Dodges

Ability Stats

  • (Base 40 + 1 Dodge Rating per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Passive: Increase Dodge Rating by 100

Veteran Killer - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

With skills learned from years of experience, every time Ezio deals Damage he will increase his Physical Attack for 4 seconds. He can further build up his Physical Attack if he deals Damage multiple times within the 4 second period

Ability Stats

  • (Base 197 + 3 Physical Attack per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Physical Attack increases by 317 when Ezio deals Damage
