Abilities

Mortal Paralysis - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Hurok Paralyzes the enemy with the highest Energy, dealing Damage-over-Time and leaving them unable to use any active ability. This ability can be triggered when Hurok's Energy is 50% or higher, but it results in Hurok rapidly burning his Energy for the rest of the wave. It cannot be used against bosses

Ability Stats

  • (Base 16 + 8 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals 816 Damage per second

Inferno - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

A firestorm deals Damage and sets enemies on fire, dealing Damage-over-Time

Ability Stats

  • (Base 21 + 11 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals 1121 Damage per second for 8 seconds
  • Attributes:

Demon's Vengence - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Hurok unleashes a ranged attack which deals Magic Damage to the enemy with the lowest Star-Level. If the target enemy is at a lower Star-Level than Hurok, additional Magic Damage will be dealt based on the difference in Star-Level, and the target will be Stunned

Ability Stats

  • (Base 440 + 20 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 2040 Damage
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time

Devour - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Hurok devours an enemy Summoned or Illusory Unit, which has the additional effect of increasing Hurok's Physical Armor, Magic Armor, and Attack Speed by 20%. The effect of using this ability multiple times can stack

Ability Stats

  • (Base 50,000 + 1000 Health per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Can devour targets with up to 110,000 Health
