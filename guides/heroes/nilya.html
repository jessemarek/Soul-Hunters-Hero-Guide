Abilities

Glacial Spikes - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Freeze the water in the air, creating 5 ice spikes that deal Magic Damage to the target. Does 2X damage if your target was Frozen before casting, Freezes your target if it was Slowed before casting, and other targets will be Slowed

Ability Stats

  • (+ 15 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1500 Damage
    Freeze and Slow Success Rates increase with every level. Enemies under level 100 will be Frozen or Slowed every time
  • Attributes:

Ice Snare - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Place an ice snare on the ground, Slowing the target and entrapping them in a tomb of ice

Ability Stats

  • (+ 1 Success Rate per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Slow Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Slowed every time
  • Attributes:

Frozen Blast - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Hurl a large spear of ice into enemy lines, dealing AoE Magic Damage and decreasing enemy Speed

Ability Stats

  • (Base 200 + 10 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 1000 Damage
  • Attributes:

Will of Frost - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Nilya's affinity for ice permanently increases her Magic Attack

Ability Stats

  • (Base 320 + 8 Magic Attack per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Passive: Increase Magic Attack by 800
