Abilities

Finishing Blow - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Tanya dashes to the enemy with the lowest Physical Attack, dealing a large base of Physical Damage. If Tanya's Physical Attack is higher than the enemy's, she will deliver additional Damage based on the difference between the two. After dealing this attack, the Damage Tanya receives will be greatly reduced for a short period

Ability Stats

  • (+ 20 Damage and Base 30 + 0.7% Additional Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 2000 Damage
    Deals 100% of the difference in Physical Attacks as Additional Damage

Whirling Daggers - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Tanya strikes an enemy with 4 quick blows, each dealing Physical Damage. The 4th strike is guaranteed to hit the enemy, and it can interrupt the ability the enemy is currently using. It also Silences the enemy for a short period of time

Ability Stats

  • (+ 7 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 700 Damage

Shattering Strike - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Tanya rushes to the enemy with the lowest Physical Attack, and weakens their Armor with a powerful slash. She does not return to her original position after using this ability and continues to fight from her new position. After dealing this Attack, the Damage Tanya receives will be greatly reduced for a short period

Ability Stats

  • (Base 200 + 10 Damage and Base 29 + 1 Physical Armor per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 1000 Damage
    Reduces enemy Physical Armor by 109

Element of Surprise - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Using the element of surprise, Tanya is able to deal additional Damage to enemies with a higher percentage of their Max Health remaining

Ability Stats

  • (Base 320 + 8 Maximum Damage and Base 80 + 2 Minimum Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Deals a maximum of 800 Additional Damage
    Deals a minimum of 200 Additional Damage

Blessed Dagger - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

All of Tanya's Attacks drain a small amount of the target's Energy. They drain more Energy when they Crit

Ability Stats

  • (Base 31.8 + 0.2 Energy and Base 63.6 + 0.4 Crit Energy per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Attacks drain 39.8 Energy
    Attacks that Crit drain 79.6 Energy
