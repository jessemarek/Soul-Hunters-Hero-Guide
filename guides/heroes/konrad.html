Abilities

Potion Master - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Whenever Konrad takes Physical Damage, he will collect Physical Essence, and whenever he takes Magic Damage he will collect Magical Essence. When a hero dies on the battlefield, Konrad will collect Energy Essence. When Potion Master is used, he creates a potion which deals severe Magic Damage to nearby enemies and may have an additional Silence, Freeze or Disarm effect based on the essence he has collected. Wile using this Ultimate, he avoids 40% of all Damage, is immune to Control Abilities, and increases the speed at which he collects Essence

Ability Stats

  • (+ 15 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1500 Damage
    Additional Effect Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be affected every time

Shattering Vial - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Throws a potion vial at an enemy, dealing Magic Damage to the target. If the target enemy has more Health remaining than Energy they will take 50% more damage

Ability Stats

  • (+ 12 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1200 Damage

Oil Slick - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Konrad's beast sprays oil at the enemy party, which deals Magic Damage and temporarily lowers the target's Magic Armor. Then Konrad uses his flamethrower, which also deals Magic Damage (extra effective against enemies previously struck by oil)

Ability Stats

  • (Base 100 + 5 Damage and Base 16 + 0.8 Magic Armor per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 500 Damage
    Reduce target's Magic Armor by 80 for 4 seconds
  • Attributes:

Power Elixir - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Konrad feeds his beast an elixir which temporarily changes his Basic Attacks from Physical to Magic and increases the duo's Physical Armor

Ability Stats

  • (Base 400 + 10 Damage and Base 78 + 2 Physical Armor per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Basic Attack deals 1000 additional Damage
    Increases Physical Armor by 198 for 15 seconds

Mutation - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

When Konrad's beast is fed the Power Elixir, the duo will now also gain a temporary increase in Magic Attack as well as regaining some Health

Ability Stats

  • (Base 608 + 10 Magic Attack and Base 602 + 10 Health per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Magic Attack increased by 1078
    Restores 1072 Health per second
