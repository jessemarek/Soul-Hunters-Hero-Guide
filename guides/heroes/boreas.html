Abilities

Frost Curse - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Boreas shoots a hailstone at the enemy party which explodes in the target area. Enemies hit by the explosion will take Damage and be affected by a Frost Curse. If a unit under the curse has less than 12% Health remaining, they will immediately take Damage equal to 20% of their Max Health, causing Instant Death. The curse also makes it harder for them to recover Health. Upon activating this ability, a target indicator will slowly move through the enemy party. Tap Boreas' avatar again to unleash the attack

Ability Stats

  • (+ 25 Explosion Damage, Base 7.9% + 0.9% Lifesteal & Health Recovery and Base 5000 + 500 Max Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Explosion deals a base of 2500 Damage
    Curse reduces Lifesteal and Health Recovery by 97.9%
    Curse can deal up to 55,000 Damage
    Curse Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Cursed every time
  • Attributes:

Icy Trap - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Boreas casts a spell which gradually Freezes an enemy, causing them to take Damage-over-Time for 4 seconds and then be Frozen. If the target enemy moves a certain distance during the 4 second period, they will avoid becoming Frozen

Ability Stats

  • (+ 15 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1500 Damage per second
    Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under evel 100 will be affected every time
  • Attributes:

Ice Vortex - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Boreas summons an icy vortex on the battlefield. Enemies that enter the vortex will have reduced Magic Armor and will recover Health more slowly

Ability Stats

  • (Base 97.5 + 3.5 Magic Armor per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Lowers Magic Armor of enemies in vortex by 377.5
  • Attributes:

Winter's Touch - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Whenever an enemy enters the ice vortex, Boreas will have increased Physical and Magic Armor. If three or more enemies are in the vortex, Boreas's Basic Attack will become more powerful, dealing AoE Damage and Slowing enemies

Ability Stats

  • (Base 160 + 4 Physical Armor and Base 100 + 2.5 Magic Armor per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Increase Physical Armor by 400 and Magic Armor by 250 for every enemy unit in vortex
