Abilities

Broken Heart - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Daphne throws a heart at the enemy party, Charming enemies who are struck by it. When the heart breaks into pieces, affected enemies suffer reduced Physical and Magic Attack.

Ability Stats

  • (+ 5 Physical Attack and + 5 Magic Attack per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Charm Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Charmed every time
    Reduces 500 Physical Attack
    Reduces Magic Attack by 500

Cheerleader - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Daphne does a dance to encourage all of her allies, increasing the Physical and Magic Armor of all friendly units. All enemy units suffer from reduced Attack Speed

Ability Stats

  • (Base 18 + 2 Physical Armor and Base 18 + 2 Magic Armor per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Increase Physical Armor by 218
    Increase Magic Armor by 218

Aerial Drop - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Daphne drops an ally into the middle of the enemy party for a few seconds, and then pulls them back to safety. The ally will temporarily have increased Magic and Physical Armor. At the same time, if there are enemy units adjacent to Daphne, they will have their current ability interrupted and they will be thrown backwards

Ability Stats

  • (Base 58 + 2 Physical Armor and Base 58 + 2 Magic Armor per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Increase Physical Armor by 218
    Increase Magic Armor by 218

Shockwave Kick - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Daphne leaps to the side of an enemy unit, and unleashes a series of kicks that deal Magic Damage. The first kick deals Single-Target Damage as well as petrifying the affected enemy. The second kick deals AoE Damage within a small area. The third kick creates a shockwave that deals Damage to any enemies it hits

Ability Stats

  • (Base 280 + 7 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Deals a base of 700 Damage
    Petrification Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Petrified every time

Spinning Top - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

Daphne uses her straps to rapidly spin an enemy unit, dealing Magic Damage. The affected enemy spins in place at first, and then moves backwards. If the enemy takes significant Damage during this period, they will spin for a longer time

Ability Stats

  • (Base 60 + 10 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Deals a base of 1000 Damage
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time
