Deceptive Assault - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Kong is reinforced by an illusion warrior that confuses and attacks the enemy, then he uses his spear to inflict a quick series of attacks which deal Physycal Damage

Frozen Spear - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Kong throws a frozen spear at an enemy, dealing Physical Damage and Slowing the target. At the same time, he summons an illusion warrior which appears next to the target
