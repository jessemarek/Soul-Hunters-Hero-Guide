Jasmine - Sun Goddess


"The shadows will be driven away!"


"The shadows will be driven away!"

Hero Info and Role

Middle Line Support: Jasmine is a powerful support hero that can Heal allies, Buff Armor, and Dispel Control Abilities placed on herself or her allies

First Appeared

Conjuring Stone

Hero Card

  • Requires 80 to summon
  • Additional Cards grant 30
  • Drops in Diamond Chest

Available In

Guild Emporium

Abilities

Illuminate - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

A brilliant light illuminates the battlefield as Jasmine simultaneously Blinds and deals Damage to the entire enemy team and Heals her allies

Ability Stats

  • (+ 5 Damage and + 5 Health per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 500 Damage per wave
    Restores a base of 500 Health per wave

Healing Light - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

After using her Basic Attack, Jasmine releases a burst of light that Heals one of her teammates

Ability Stats

  • (+ 3 Health per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Restores a base of 300 Health

Solar Halo - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Jasmine creates a shield of light to protect herself or one of her allies. The shield absorbs some Physical and Magic Damage

Ability Stats

  • (Base 630 + 30 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Absorbs up to 3030 Damage

Reflected Beam - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Boost the effect that Healing Spells have on your allies as well dispelling all Control Abilities and debuffs affecting your teammates. Alternatively, Jasmine can use this ability to decrease the effect that Healing Spells have on her enemies as well as removing any buffs

Ability Stats

  • (Base 11.7 + 0.3% Effective on allies and Base 16.7 + 0.3% Effective on enemies per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Healing abilities are 29.7% more effective on allies
    Healing abilities are 34.7% less effective on enemies
    Dispell abilities cast by heroes under level 100
