Abilities

Intrepid Charge - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Valan charges ahead and collides with the enemy with the highest remaining Health. This Attack cannot be Dodged and will Lift the target enemy into the air. When the target enemy lands, other nearby enemy units will take Physical Damage and be Stunned

Ability Stats

  • (+ 25 Collision Damage and + 30 Impact Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Collision deals a base of 2500 Damage
    Impact from landing deals a base of 3000 Damage
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time

Stomping Hooves - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Valan stomps on the earth, dealing Physical Damage to nearby enemies, Knocking them back, and momentarily Stunning them

Ability Stats

  • (+ 15 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1500 Damage
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time

Unyielding Shield - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Valan provides himself and his allies with air shields that absorb a fixed amount of Damage

Ability Stats

  • (Base 1950 + 100 Damage Absorbed per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Absorbs up to 9950 Damage

Warrior of Iron - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Valan's massive size not only makes him a formidable attacker, it also shields his teammates from harm. Valan passively gives a Physical Armor boost to himself and the ally with the lowest Physical Armor

Ability Stats

  • (Base 200 + 5 per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Passive: Valan and teammate increase Physical Armor by 500

Persistent Protection - Unlocks with Knight's Armor Skin

Ability Description

The shields created by Valan's ability will last an additional 3 seconds. If one of the shields is broken by an enemy attack, the hero who had the shield will get a large Speed increase for 3 seconds

Ability Info

Ability only available while Knight's Armor Skin is equipped
