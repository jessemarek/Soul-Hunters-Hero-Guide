Abilities

Airstrike - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

The Goblin Squad calls for air support, summoning two units that fly over the enemy party and attack with lasers, each of them dealing Single-Target Magic Damage. Tapping the Squad's avatar again will order the air support to divebomb the enemy, which deals Damage in proportion to the amount of time they have been flying. If the ability is interrupted, the divebomb attack will be triggered immediately

Ability Stats

  • (+ 12 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1200 Damage
  • Attributes:

Proximity Mine - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

The Goblin Squad deploys a footsoldier to run across the battlefield, placing mines along the way. Mines will explode when an enemy gets too close or after a certain amount of time, dealing AoE Magic Damage

Ability Stats

  • (+ 15 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1500 Damage
  • Attributes:

Explosive Surprise - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

The Goblin Squad leader throws a box which opens when it hits the ground, releasing either a mine that deals AoE Magic Damage or an EMP device that can Stun enemies. EMP devices can be triggered at a greater distance than mines

Ability Stats

  • (Base 360 + 18 per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 1800 Damage
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time

Ultimate Sacrifice - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

The Goblin Squad charges recklessly at the enemy and detonates their explosives, vowing to destroy their enemies at the cost of their own lives. Enemies within range take a huge amount of Magic Damage. This ability is only triggered when the Squad has less than 20% Health remaining

Ability Stats

  • (Base 1600 + 40 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Deals a base of 4000 Damage
  • Attributes:
