Abilities

Spotlight - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Under the spotlight, Solomon performs his signature trick, transforming a random enemy into a weak minion for a short period. Damage taken by the minion remains when the unit reverts to its original form. This ability is ineffective against bosses of all types

Ability Stats

  • (+ 1 Success Rate per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be affected every time

Enigma Box - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Solomon summons a mysterious magic box. If the box is struck by an enemy within a few seconds, it will explode, dealing Magic Damage. Otherwise, it will release a dove which allows teammates to recover Health and increase their Physical and Magic Attack. The dove Buffs allies twice before disappearing

Ability Stats

  • (+ 12 Damage, Base 25 + 5 Magic Attack and Base 25 + 5 Physical Attack per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Explosion deals a base of 1200 Damage
    Dove increases Magic Attack by 525
    Dove increases Physical Attack by 525

Flaming Cap - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Solomon uses magic to ignite his hat, then throws it at the enemy party, dealing AoE Magic Damage

Ability Stats

  • (Base 100 + 5 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 500 Damage
  • Attributes:

Blinding Light - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Solomon shoots a blinding light from his wand at a single enemy, dealing Magic Damage multiple times and Blinding the target

Ability Stats

  • (Base 320 + 8 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Deals a base of 800 Damage
  • Attributes:

Wild Card - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

Solomon creates a shield which appears for a short time, protecting each his teammates from a single Attack of any type. If the shield blocks Damage, allies will recover Health and increase their Physical and Magic Attack

Ability Stats

  • (Base 3150 + 50 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Absorbs up to 5150 Damage
