Blade Dash - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Dash into enemy lines and deliver a series of rapid devastating blows to multiple enemies

Ability Stats

Ninja Slash - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Deal a quick and accurate slash attack, dealing Physical Damage and Stunning the target

Ability Stats

  • (+ 10 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1000 Damage
    Chance of Stun increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time

Backhand Slice - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Perform a spinning attack that deals Physical Damage and lifts the target into the air

    Lift Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be lifted every time
