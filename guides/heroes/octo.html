Abilities

Bounding Leaps - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Octo makes a series of bounding leaps around the enemy party. Each time he lands, he deals Physical Damage to surrounding enemies. With each leap, he gains more and more Health, his Attacks cause more Damage and temporarily increases his Movement and Attack Speed

Ability Stats

  • (+ 18 Damage, + 35 Health and + 0.04% Attack Speed per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1800 Damage
    Recover x Health with each leap
    Gets extra 4% Attack Speed with every leap

Aquatic Shield - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Octo creates a wall of water that temporarily reduces the amount of Physical Damage he takes. When the wall disappears, all Debuffs and Control Abilities will be removed from Octo and nearby enemies will take Physical Damage

Ability Stats

  • (+ 10 Damage and + 0.35% Physical Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1000 Damage
    Physical Damage reduced by 35%

Harpoon Strike - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Octo uses his swordfish as a harpoon, rapidly stabbing the enemy party. Deals AoE Physical Damage and lowers enemy Physical Armor

Ability Stats

  • (Base 340 + 17 Damage and Base 16 + 0.8 Physical Armor per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 1700 Damage
    Lowers enemy's Physical Armor by 80 for 8 seconds

Pufferfish Rain - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Octo reels in a school of pufferfish that attack the enemy, dealing Physical Damage and reducing enemy Attack Speed and Movement Speed

Ability Stats

  • (Base 600 + 15 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Deals a base of 1500 Damage
    Slow Success Rate increases with ability level. enemies under level 100 will be Slowed every time

Energy Swap - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

Octo's Basic Attack as well as his Pufferfish Rain and Harpoon Strike Abilities will steal some of the target's Physical Attack. The Physical Attack boost is temporary, but up to 15 layers can accumulate

Ability Stats

  • (Base 128 + 2 Attack per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Each layer steals 208 of enemy's Attack
