Desmond - Bionic Bat

"Weapons fully charged!"

Middle Line DPS: Flies on a hoverboard and uses burning oil to deal Damage-over-Time to his enemies

Abilities

Fireball Volley - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Desmond elevates his hoverboard to a higher altitude, increasing his Dodge rating. Then he dives at the enemy formation, shooting fireballs that deal Magic Damage to random enemies. As Desmond is returning to his side of the battlefield, he will drag an enemy that direction with him. After being released, the affected enemy will be Stunned

Ability Stats

  • (+ 20 Damage, + 4 Dodge Rating per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Fireballs deal 2000 Damage
    Increases Dodge Rating by 400
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time
  • Attributes:

Flamethrower - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Desmond uses his flamethrower to unleash a blast of green flame at the enemy party, dealing AoE Magic Damage

Ability Stats

  • (+ 17 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Flame deals 1700 Damage
  • Attributes:

Oil Drum - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Desmond throws an oil drum at his enemies, who become covered in sticky oil. When struck by Desmond's other attacks, affected enemies will take additional Damage-over-Time due to the oil burning. Enemies can be struck by oil multiple times, and the effects are cumulative.

Ability Stats

  • (Base 124 + 6 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Burning oil deals 604 Damage per second for 6 seconds

Remote Detonation - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Desmond flies a remote-control explosive device into the midst of the enemy party, which explodes and deals AoE Magic Damage. The blast can interrupt enemy abilities and scatters enemy units

Ability Stats

  • (Base 600 + 15 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Explosion deals a base of 1500 Damage
    Interrupt Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Interrupted every time
  • Attributes:

Drone Strike - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

Desmond summons a bat-drone which assists him in battle. One type of drone can increase his Magic Attack, while another type sprays flammable liquid on enemy units, causing them to be more susceptible to Damage-over-Time dealt by Desmond's other abilities

Ability Stats

  • (Base 600 + 10 Magic Attack per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Increases Magic Attack by 1000
