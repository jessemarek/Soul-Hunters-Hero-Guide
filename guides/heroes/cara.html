Abilities

Cage of Eternity - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Cara traps an enemy in a cage and inflicts a curse on them, dealing Magic Damage-over-Time and Stunning them. While this Ultimate is being used, Cara is immune to all Control Abilities

Ability Stats

  • (+ 18 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1800 Damage
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time

Kiss of Death - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Cara kisses an enemy, cursing them with greatly reduced Physical Attack and Freezing them for a short period

Ability Stats

  • (+ 20 Physical Attack per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Reduces target's Physical Attack by 2000
    Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be affected every time

Flower Beam - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Cara shoots a laser beam that deals Magic Damage to a single enemy. Cara will gain as much Health as the enemy loses

Ability Stats

  • (Base 500 + 25 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 2500 Damage

Nightmare - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Cara hypnotizes the enemy into a dream-state, and they are Frozen until they take an Attack. If they are not awakened by an Attack, the dream-state will transfer to another enemy. The dream-state may be transferred twice at most

Ability Stats

  • (Base 40 + 1 Success Rate per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be affected every time

Dream Potion - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

The first time Cara is near death, she will take a potion which puts her into a deep sleep for 5 seconds. While sleeping, she is immune to Damage and recovers some Health

Ability Stats

  • (Base 8 + 0.05% Health per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Recover 10% Health per second
