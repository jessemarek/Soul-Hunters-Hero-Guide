Abilities

All In - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Where other Heroes would gain Energy, Dalton gains poker chips. When he has 7 or more chips, this Ultimate may be activated. Dalton bets on which surviving enemy Hero has dealt the most Damage. If he guesses correctly, all the chips will be cleared, the selected enemy will take Magic Damage based on how many chips Dalton has, and Dalton will gain Health equivalent to 40% of the Damage dealt. If he guesses incorrectly, he will lose half his chips. When auto-battle is activated, his chances of guessing correctly depend on how much Damage the most effective enemy unit dealt. When battling manually, select an enemy by tapping on Dalton's avatar again when that unit's image appears

Ability Stats

  • (Base 105 + 25 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 2605 Damage

Roll of the Dice - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Dalton and the enemy unit with the lowest Magic Attack each roll a die, whoever rolls the lower number will take Magic Damage and will be momentarily Stunned. If Dalton wins, he will earn one chip; if he loses, he will lose one chip. He has a chance of rolling an unbeatable "7", the probability is determined by the difference between Dalton's and the target's Magic Attack

Ability Stats

  • (Base 45 + 15 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1545 Damage
    Chance of Stun increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time

Russian Roulette - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

When Dalton takes damage that would normally kill him, he will have 1 HP remaining. He then gambles all his chips and makes a final bet with the enemy that dealt the Damage. He spins his gun, with a 50% chance of shooting the enemy and dealing Damage, and a 50% of shooting himself and losing the last of his Health. The more chips that he has, the more Damage this attack potentially deals. If the attack deals Damage to the enemy, Dalton will gain Health equivalent to 40% of the Damage dealt by this Attack. This Attack may only be used once per wave

Ability Stats

  • (Base 660 + 30 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 3060 Damage

Three of a Kind - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Dalton will remember the last three numbers he rolled when using the ability "Roll of the Dice". If two of the last three numbers match, Dalton will get 2 chips, and will restore Health to his weakest ally. If all three numbers match, he will earn 3 chips and the Health Recovery effect will be doubled. If he rolls three 7s in a row, the next time he wins "Roll of the Dice", the enemy will be Silenced for 6 seconds

Ability Stats

  • (Base 810 + 20 Health per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Restores 2010 Health

Comeback Kid - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

The lower Dalton's Health, the higher his Magic Attack and Magic Pierce Rating. These stats are at a maximum when Dalton is at 30% Health or less

Ability Stats

  • (Base 1200 + 20 Magic Attack and Base 420 + 4.5 Magic Armor per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Increases Magic Attack by a max of 2000
    Ignores a maximum of 600 Magic Armor
