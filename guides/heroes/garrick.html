Abilities

Dead or Alive - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

A random "prize" is placed on the head of every enemy, the hero who kills them will get the prize. The prizes are of 5 types: Health, Energy, Physical Attack, Physical Shield and Magic Shield. Affected enemies take double the Damage from Basic Attacks and may not become invisible. The ability also increases the Attack Speed of allies

Ability Stats

  • (+ 100 Health, Base 360 + 4 Energy, + 30 Physical Attack, + 100 Physical Damage and + 100 Magic Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Health Prize: 10000
    Energy Prize: 760
    Physical Attack Prize: 3000
    Physical Shield Prize: Blocks 10000 Damage
    Magic Shield Prize: Blocks 10000 Damage

Invisibility - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Garrick becomes invisible and cannot be selected as the target for Single-Target enemy abilities. Using a Basic Attack ends Garrick's invisibility, but the Basic Attack deals Additional Damage

Ability Stats

  • (Base 14.6 + 0.7% per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Increases Basic Attack by 84.6%

Flying Shuriken - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Throw a ninja star at the enemy party dealing Physical Damage and Stunning enemies it hits for short period

Ability Stats

  • (Base 300 + 15 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 1500 Damage
    Stun Success rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time

Disciplined Killer - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Garrick's Physical Attack gradually builds up over time, eventually being released and providing a large Damage Increase to a Basic Attack

Ability Stats

  • (Base 240 + 6 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Increase Physical Attack by 600 per second

Fugitive's Fear - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

The lower the Health of enemy Heroes Garrick has marked with his Ultimate, the more easily they will take Damage. Against Heroes with very high Max Health, the effectiveness will be reduced by 40%

Ability Stats

  • (Base 6 + 0.1% Minimum Damage and Base 27 + 0.45% Maximum Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Deals a minimum of 10% additional Damage
    Deals a maximum of 45% additional Damage

Defense System - Unlocks with Soldier of Anarchy Skin

Ability Description

When an enemy unit gets close to Garrick, there is a chance of activating a defense system which Stuns the enemy unit for 2.5 seconds (ineffective against illusionary or summoned units)

Ability Info

Ability only available while Soldier of Anarchy Skin is equipped
