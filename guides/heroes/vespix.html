Abilities

Perfect Storm - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Vespix summons two whirlwinds that sweep across the battlefield, lifting enemies and dealing AOE Physical Damage. If enemies had previously been struck by Slow Poison, they will take additional Physical Damage

Ability Stats

  • (+ 12 Damage and + 20 Additional Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1200 Damage
    Deals 2000 additional Damage if affected by Slow Poison
    Whirlwind Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be affected every time

Slow Poison - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Vespix spits toxin at an enemy which Stuns the enemy and has several delayed effects. At random times throughout the battle, the affected enemy will have their abilities interrupted, lose Energy and Health, and take Physical Damage. They will also be more susceptible to Vespix's other abilities. Vespix's priority target will be a nearby enemy who is not yet affected by the toxin

Ability Stats

  • (+ 12 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Periodically deals 1200 Damage
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time

Slashing Hooks - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Vespix singles out an enemy and deals a deadly blow with both his hooks, dealing Physical Damage. If the enemy is affected by Slow Poison, they will take twice the Damage

Ability Stats

  • (Base 240 + 12 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 1200 Damage

Rampage - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Vespix sprints into the enemy party and repeatedly strikes one or more enemies within range. While using this ability, Vespix's protective shell allows him to minimize the amount of Damage he takes

Ability Stats

  • (Base 400 + 10 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Deals a base of 1000 Damage

Side Effect - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

Whenever Slow Poison depletes enemy Health, Vespix will regain some Health and Energy

Ability Stats

  • (Base 600 + 10 Health per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Restores a base of 1000 Health
