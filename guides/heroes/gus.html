Abilities

Panda Smash - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Gus smashes the ground, causing the earth to rise up beneath 4 of his enemies, lifting them and dealing Physical Damage. The further the enemy is from Gus, the more Damage they receive

Ability Stats

  • (+ 25 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 2500 Damage
    Lift Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Lifted every time

Disarming Strike - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Gus flings 3 stones, each of which deals Physical Damage to an enemy and Disarms them (making them unable to use Physical Attacks for a short period)

Ability Stats

  • (+ 16 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1600 Damage
    Disarm Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Disarmed every time

Rolling Boulder - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Gus transforms into a stone ball and rolls through the enemy party, dealing Physical Damage to any enemy he hits. He then places a Magic Pillar, and flies back to his original position

Ability Stats

  • (Base 240 + 12 Damage and Base 100 + 5 Physical Attack per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 1200 Damage
    Places a level 100 Magic Pillar
    Reduces enemy Physical Attack by 500

Magic Pillar - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Gus places a magic pillar on the battlefield which has the effect of lowering the Physical Attack of all enemies on the field. Multiple pillars may be on the battlefield at once. Once the pillars have been placed, you may tap Gus' portrait to detonate them, dealing AoE Physical Damage. In the arena, once 3 pillars have been placed on the battlefield, they will explode automatically

Ability Stats

  • (Base 200 + 5 Physical Attack per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Places a level 100 Magic Pillar
    Reduces enemy Physical Attack by 500
