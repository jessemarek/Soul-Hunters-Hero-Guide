Abilities

Meteor Shower - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Invokes a hail of meteors that deal Magic Damage and Blind the entire enemy party

Ability Stats

  • (+ 15 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1500 Damage
    Blind Success Rate increses with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Blinded every time

Tiger Leap - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

At the beginning of a wave, leap into battle and encourage your allies. Allies will all recover a small amount of Energy and will have increased Movement and Attack Speed for a short period

Ability Stats

  • (Base 29.5 + 0.5 Energy per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Restores 79.5 Energy

Sacred Arrow - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Enemies hit by this arrow will take Damage and be Stunned for a extended period

Ability Stats

  • (Base 200 + 10 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 1000 Damage
    Stun Success Rate increses with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time

Night Offensive - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Moonlight shines down upon Embrael and her allies, increasing the entire team's Physical Pierce Rating

Ability Stats

  • (Base 60 + 1.5 Pierce Rating per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Aura: All allies increase Physical Pierce Rating by 150

Cold Vengeance - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

Every time one of her allies falls, Embrael encourages her remaining teammates to fight on. In addition to the usual effects of "Tiger Leap" allies will also have increased Physical and Magic Attack

Ability Stats

  • ((Base 120 + 2 Physical Attack and Base 180 + 3 Magic Attack per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: All allies increase Physical Attack by 200
    Temporarily increase Magic Attack of allies by 300
