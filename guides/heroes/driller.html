Abilities

Scalding Steam - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Driller releases a blast of steam which deals Physical Damage to nearby enemies and Stuns them for a short period. This Ultimate can be used when Driller's energy is at 50% or higher

Ability Stats

  • (+ 12 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1200 Damage
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time

Underground Ambush - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Driller drills underground, then ambushes the enemy with the highest Magic Attack from below. The enemy will take Physical Damage and then be Stunned, and nearby enemies will be Knocked back

Ability Stats

  • (+ 15 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1500 Damage
    Knock-back Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Knocked back every time

Missile Rain - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Driller fires a pair of missiles which can split into 3 smaller explosives which then rain down on the entire enemy party. Each explosive deals damage over a small area. If a single enemy is hit by two or more explosives, they will be Stunned

Ability Stats

  • (Base 100 + 5 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Each explosive deals a base of 500 Damage
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time

Armor-Plating - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Driller enjoys the protection of heavy Armor plates. The lower Driller's remaining Health, the more protection they provide against Magic and Physical Attacks

Ability Stats

  • (Base 28 + 0.7% Physical Damage and Base 28 + 0.7% Magic Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Passive: Physical Damage taken reduced by 70%
    Passive: Magic Damage taken reduced by 70%
