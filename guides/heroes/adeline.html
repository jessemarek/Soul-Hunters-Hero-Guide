Abilities

Breaking Free - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Adeline breaks free of her strings, goes to the rear of the enemy party, and delivers a series of Attacks. She will then lose some Health and self-destruct, dealing additional Magic Damage to nearby enemies. If she dies while her Ultimate is activated, Adeline will also self-destruct, dealing a slightly lower amount of Damage. Adeline is immune to the majority of Control Abilities

Ability Stats

  • (+ 26 Damage and + 33 Self-Destruct Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 2600 Damage
    Self-destruct deals 3300 Damage

Magic Bolt - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Adeline casts a spell that deals Damage to a distant enemy. The greater the distance, the greater the Damage dealt. The affected enemy will also be temporarily Slowed

Ability Stats

  • (+ 25 Maximum Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a maximum of 2500 Damage
  • Attributes:

Song of Chaos - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Using her reed-pipe, Adeline steals the soul of a distant enemy, causing that enemy to lose Health and Energy, some of which is transferred to Adeline. For a certain period after the initial Attack, any time Adeline takes Damage, some of it will be transferred to the affected enemy

Ability Stats

  • (Base 400 + 20 Damage and Base 75.7 + 0.8 Energy Drain per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 2000 Damage
    Enemy unit loses 139.7 Energy

Spike Strip - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Adeline causes spikes to come out of the ground near the Back-Line of the enemy party, Stunning enemies in the area and dealing Magic Damage

Ability Stats

  • (Base 480 + 12 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Deals a base of 1200 Damage
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time
