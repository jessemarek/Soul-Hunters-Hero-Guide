Abilities

Life Force - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Lumos merges with a teammate for the rest of the wave, giving them 30% of his Magic Attack, Magic Crit Rating, and Physical Armor, as well as boosting their Attack Speed. At the same time, Lumos Stuns enemies close to the recipient teammate. When the teammate is about to take a mortal blow, Lumos can take the hit for them, as well as restoring some Health to the teammate. Lumos may not merge with heroes who have revival or suicide abilities and cannot block Damage-over-Time

Ability Stats

  • (+ 20 Damage and 0.5% Health per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 2000 Damage
    Ally gains Health equivalent to 50% of Lumos' Health
    Knock-back Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Knocked back every time

Chain of Light - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Lumos establishes a link with one of his allies, giving both Lumos and the ally a 10% reduction in Magic and Physical Damage taken. Both heroes also get a boost in Health. While the heroes are linked, the ally will get a temporary boost in Attack Speed whenever Lumos uses one of his abilities

Ability Stats

  • (Base 15 + 5 Health and 0.25% Attack Speed per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Restores 515 Health per second
    Increase Attack Speed by 25%

Spirit's Rage - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Lumos casts a spell on enemies within range, dealing AoE Magic Damage as well as Damage-over-Time

Ability Stats

  • (Base 300 + 15 Damage and Base 60 + 3 Damage per second per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 1500 Damage
    Deals 300 Damage per seconds for 12 seconds

Fragmentation - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Lumos divides himself into multiple fragments, the main spirit becoming invincible for a short time. The fragments each serve as a shield for a teammate, blocking a single attack. When they block an attack, Lumos will receive a boost to his Energy

Ability Stats

  • (Base 2050 + 50 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: One fragment can block up to 5050 Damage
