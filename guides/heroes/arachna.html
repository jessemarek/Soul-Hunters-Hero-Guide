Abilities

Noxious Cloud - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Release a noxious cloud of venom, dealing Magic Damage to the nearest enemy, also additional Damage-over-Time. If the target dies from the cloud, it will spread to the nearest target, resetting the timer on the spell's Damage-over-Time

Ability Stats

  • (+ 6 Damage and Base 38 + 12 Damage per second per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 600 Damage
    Deals a base of 1238 Damage per second for 6 seconds

Poison Glob - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Hurl a poison blob at an enemy, deals Magic Damage and additional Damage-over-Time

Ability Stats

  • (+ 8 Damage and Base 17 + 3 Damage per second per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 800 Damage
    Deals a base of 317 Damage per second for 2 seconds

Web Snare - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Throw a web, dealing Magic Damage. Enemies hit by the web can't move, Dodge, or use any Physical Attacks

Ability Stats

  • (Base 80 + 4 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 400 Damage
    Chance of causing Confusion increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Confused every time

Toxic Spine - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Defend yourself with a toxic spine, applying a Damage-over-Time effect to enemies who hit you with a Physical Attack

Ability Stats

  • (Base 342 + 8 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Deals a base of 822 Damage per second for 5 seconds

Potent Venom - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

Damage-over-Time dealt by Arachna's abilities will be increased on targets with less than 50% of their Max Health

Ability Stats

  • (Base 74.5 + 0.5% per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Damage-over-Time increased by 94.5%
