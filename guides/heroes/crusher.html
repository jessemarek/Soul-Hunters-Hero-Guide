Crusher - Stone Colossus


"It's time to Rock 'n' Roll!"


"It's time to Rock 'n' Roll!"

Hero Info and Role

Front Line Tank: Crusher can control the battlefield by draining the Energy of enemy Heroes, as well as using defensive abilities to protect himself and his allies

First Appeared

Conjuring Stone

Hero Card

  • Requires 80 to summon
  • Additional Cards grant 30
  • Drops in Diamond Chest

Available In

Legendary Shop

Abilities

Colossal Wrath - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Crusher rushes at the enemy with the highest Energy, delivering a deadly blow which lifts them into the air and depletes a large amount of their Energy

Ability Stats

  • (+ 78.4 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 7840 Damage

Smashing Fists - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Crusher strikes the Front Line enemy with his powerful granite fists, dealing significant Physical Damage and Stunning the enemy for a short period

Ability Stats

  • (+ 16 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 800 Damage

Rock Solid - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Crusher transforms into a ball, reducing his susceptibility to enemy attacks, at the same time depleting Energy from enemies who strike him

Ability Stats

  • (Base 21.9 + 0.1% Physical Damage and Base + 0.1% Magic Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Reduce Physical Damage received by 29.9%
    Reduce Magic Damage received by 29.9%

Granite Shield - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Crusher grants protection to the ally with the lowest Health. The affected ally will be immune to a certain amount of Damage for 10 seconds as well as recovering a small amount of Energy when struck by enemies. When Crusher is killed in battle, he will summon the shield a final time with his dying breath

Ability Stats

  • (Base 2615 + 65 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Absorbs up to 6515 Damage for 10 seconds
