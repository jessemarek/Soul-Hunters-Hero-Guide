Abilities

Ghosts of the Ancients - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Bloodspear invokes the spirits of the ancients to help his entire party increase their Physical and Magic Attack as well as their Attack Speed

Ability Stats

  • (+ 10 Physical Attack and + 10 Magic Attack per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Increase Physical Attack by 1000 for 10 seconds
    Increase Magic Attack by 1000 for 10 seconds

Blessed Spear - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Bloodspear throws a blessed spear at a single enemy, dealing a massive amount of Physical Damage. If Bloodspear's 4th ability is unlocked, Blessed Spear will place the Curse of Destruction twice on the target

Ability Stats

  • (+ 14 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 1400 Damage

Shield Smash - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Bloodspear delivers a vicious hit with his shield, knocking enemies back and dealing Physical Damage

Ability Stats

  • (Base 240 + 12 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 1200 Damage
    Knock-back Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Knocked back every time

Curse of Destruction - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Every time Bloodspear hits an enemy, the enemy is afflicted with a curse making them more susceptible to Bloodspear's next attack. The curse can be inflicted up to 5 times per enemy, each time increasing the Damage the will receive from Bloodspear's next Attack

Ability Stats

  • (Base 6.95 + 0.05% per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Damage inceased by 9.95% each time enemy is hit
