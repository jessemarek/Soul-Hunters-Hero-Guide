Abilities

Double Strike - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Kaneq rolls through the enemy party in a giant snowball, headed for the enemy with the highest Physical Attack. Enemies he hits on the way will take Physical Damage and be Stunned. Then he slaps the target with a giant fish, dealing a large amount of Physical Damage and lifting them. While rolling through the enemy party, Kaneq is immune to all Control Abilities. If the target enemy is destroyed before he arrives, Kaneq will automatically select another target

Ability Stats

  • (Base 91 + 9 Snowball Damage and + 50 Fish Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Snowball deals a base of 991 Damage
    Fish deals a base of 4951 Damage
  • Attributes:

Snowball Volley - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Kaneq and his penguins throw a volley of snowballs at a random target, dealing Physical Damage and Slowing the enemy. The snowball thrown by Kaneq has an additional lifting effect

Ability Stats

  • (+ 6 Damage and Base 0.1 + 0.2% Attack Speed per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Deals a base of 600 Damage
    Reduces enemy Attack Speed by 20.1%
  • Attributes:

Penguin Assassin - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Kaneq dispatches a penguin to place an ice bomb behind enemy lines, targeting the enemy with the highest Physical Attack. When the bomb explodes, it deals Physical Damage, Knocks-back and Slows the target enemy, as well as other enemies in the vicinity

Ability Stats

  • (Base 246 + 12 Damage and Base 4.3 + 0.2% Attack Speed per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Deals a base of 1206 Damage
    Reduces enemy Attack Speed by 20.3%
  • Attributes:

Frostbite - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Whenever a fixed amount of time elapses, Kaneq's next Attack is guaranteed to Crit

Ability Stats

  • (Base 4 + 0.1 Reduction per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Attacks will Crit every 10.1 seconds

Flying Penguin - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Description

Kaneq periodically throws a penguin at the enemy party, dealing Physical Damage to a single enemy. In addition, if the enemy's Physical Attack is higher than their Magic Attack, they will be Charmed. If their Magic Attack is higher, they will be Silenced
