Abilities

Dark Guardian - Ultimate Ability

Ability Description

Avior comes to the aid of his teammates, absorbing most of the Damage that would have been taken by his allies for a short period, and deflecting some of the Damage back at the enemy when the Ultimate ends. While using this Ultimate, Avior has all 4 layers of Dragonscale Armor (4th ability), and is immune to Control abilities. If Avior is hit by what would be a lethal attack while using this Ultimate, the Damage will be blocked and deflected back at an enemy unit immediately

Ability Stats

  • (Base 450 + 50 Transfered Damage and + 20 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Transfers up to 5450 Damage from each allied hero to himself
    Deals a base of 2000 Deflection Damage

Aerial Ally - Unlocks with Green Fusion

Ability Description

Avior summons a dragon ally that hovers over the enemy back line, launching attacks which deal Magic Damage and interrupting enemy abilities. The summoned dragon only exists for a short time before exploding, which also deals Magic Damage and Stuns enemies in the immediate vicinity

Ability Stats

  • (+ 1 Level per Ability Point)
  • Level 100: Summons a level 100 Dargon Ally
    Stun Success Rate increases with ability level. Enemies under level 100 will be Stunned every time

Ghosts of the Fallen - Unlocks with Blue Fusion

Ability Description

Avior feasts on the Health lost by enemies and allies alike, strengthening his Basic Attack. This ability has three tiers; a new tier is activated every time any unit takes a significant amount of Damage. The 1st Tier turns his Basic Attacks from Physical to Magic. The 2nd Tier gives his Basic Attacks additional Damage and increases his Attack Speed. The 3rd Tier allows his Basic Attack to fire an additional projectile

Ability Stats

  • (Base 360 + 18 Damage per Ability Point)
  • Level 80: Basic Attack deals 1800 additional Damage when 2nd Tier activated

Dragonscale Armor - Unlocks with Purple Fusion

Ability Description

Avior relies on his tough dragon-scales to protect him, giving him increased Physical and Magic Armor. By default, he has one layer of dragon-scales which cannot be destroyed, and he will grow up to 3 additional layers over time. Each layer can withstand at least 4 hits before breaking (the layers also heal themselves over time)

Ability Stats

  • (Base 40 + 1 Physical Armor and Base 40 + 1 Magic Armor per Ability Point)
  • Level 60: Each layer increases Physical Armor by 100
    Each layer increases Magic Armor by 100

Grip of Mortality - Unlocks with Awakening

Ability Stats

  • (Base 98.5 + 1.5 per Ability Point)
  • Level 40: Deals 158.5 Damage over a period of 8 seconds
